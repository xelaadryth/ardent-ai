# Description

A viscous toxin refined from the venom sacs of the predatory cremwidow.

# Stats

- Tier 2 Consumable (Poison)
- Cost: 150 marks per dose
- Usage: 1 action to coat up to 3 arrows, bolts, or one melee weapon.
- Effect: The target is Restrained. Each round the target may test Strength or Willpower (DC 14) to break free.
- Duration: 1 hour

Created by [[Fina]] on the way to Revolar.
