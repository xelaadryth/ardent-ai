# Karani

## Appearance

Dark brown eyes and a short, stocky Alethi build. Karani has a somewhat "unrefined" appearance, taking manly, heavy steps and often appearing nervous. She wears simple darkeyed clothing, often stained with ink.

## Personality

Shy, scared, and a bit of a people-pleaser. Karani is an outsider who feels out of place on Roshar (coming from another world like the party). She is easily frightened and makes frequent mistakes due to her nerves, leading her to avoid danger whenever possible.

## Background

Like the players, Karani is a worldhopper brought to Roshar by the Truthkeepers. Feeling unprotected and vulnerable, she was approached by the Ghostbloods, who offered her better safety in exchange for her assistance.

## Goals & Motivations

* **Safety:** To find a place where she isn't constantly in danger or being bullied.

* **Ghostblood Support:** She is helping the Ghostbloods in the hopes that they will keep her safe. She has learned to use a spanreed to facilitate communication for them.

## Connections

* [[npc_chip_of_the_ghostbloods|Chip]]**&#x20;:** Her primary contact and the one she "sticks with" for protection.

* [[npc_tahs|Tahs]]**&#x20;:** Her primary tormentor within the Truthkeeper camp.

* [[faction_ghostbloods|Ghostbloods]]**&#x20;:** Her secret patrons.

## Secrets

She is a worldhopper who has already "flipped" and is actively reporting to the Ghostbloods via spanreed.
