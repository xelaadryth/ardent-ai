## The Conflict

The party travels to Jah Keved to uncover the origin of the unstable [[kA7mYskuyJPSTksWkIdEY|Nurian Gems]]. This journey is fueled by the Nurian party members' curiosity about hearing about their own world, [[Sbddxxcp_K83M9wuLSnYd|N'tal]]'s thirst for revenge against the [[RPZ-tfzIqzRhfboC6cgfN|Bloodmarks]], and [[w6fnDcVAiSSqRF5bZrXsW|Kuma]]'**s** obsession with crafting and artifabrials. However, they arrive in a nation gripped by paranoia as [[HQd0CMtjNfK5GQh_YnXwn|Szeth-son-Neturo]] the Assassin in White begins his slaughter of Veden highprinces.

## Key Players

- [[Sbddxxcp_K83M9wuLSnYd|N'tal]]: Seeking the Grand Justicar of the [[RPZ-tfzIqzRhfboC6cgfN|Bloodmarks]] responsible for his family's murder.
    
- [[w6fnDcVAiSSqRF5bZrXsW|Kuma]]: Focused on the artifabrial demonstration and the Thaylen steamship project.
    
- [[BiRzGzdsWj4sKDLB0pjdu|Vorn]]: Leader of the Thaylen steamship project.
    
- [[-Rxr_nUPzXkMKZKBG7hGw|Ilsh]]: A spren researcher recruited to help with the project.
    
- [[VQDn-B-QY-Wud7NEa9o6R|Grand Justicar Malice]] : A high-ranking Bloodmark official in [[JlRniRHuHoKcG8CVD1tbZ|Bodenar]] who controls the Nurian gem trade.
    
- [[AbDnjy7tS_jZgjDlxKjPC|Undertaker]] **(Mraize)**: Directing the [[ZOWhgc_9NJz8OMvKu3X6o|Ghostbloods]] to investigate the highprince assassinations and maintain stability.
    
- [[HQd0CMtjNfK5GQh_YnXwn|Szeth-son-son-Vallano]] : The phantom threat whose actions cast suspicion on all Shin "outsiders".
    

## Stakes

- **N'tal's Vengeance**: Failure to navigate the politics of Bodenar could result in N'tal being branded a criminal or "Possessed" by the very people he seeks to kill.
    
- **Technological Stability**: If the steamship's Nurian core is sabotaged or fails, it could trigger a disaster that destroys the Bodenar docks.
    
- **Political Collapse**: The death of the highprinces threatens to plunge Jah Keved into a civil war that would disrupt Ghostblood trade and research.
    

## Progression

**The Journey West**: Navigating the Alethi border into Jah Keved, where the party's "outsider" status makes them prime suspects for harboring the Assassin in White.

**The Bodenar Arrival**: Meeting with the engineers and witnessing the preparations for the [[ZnWqx2eksygu5Fq-PWoD-|Experimental Steamship (Scene)]] and [[6kzdZHrPAL5rTUle3BFoA|The Steamship's Secret (Secret)]]

**The Bloodmark Infiltration**: N'tal tracks the Nurian gem shipments back to the Grand Justicar's estate.

**The Trial and the Trap**: As the steamship begins its run, the Grand Justicar moves to eliminate the "meddling worldhoppers," while rumors of a nearby highprince's death send the city into lockdown.
