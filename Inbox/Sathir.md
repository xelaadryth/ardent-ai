# Highlady Marise Sathir

## Appearance

A woman of intense focus and strict bearing, with pale green eyes. She is in her mid-20s and dresses elegantly in robes that show signs of careful repair—hand-sewn embroidery covering small tears, visible only to an expert. She often squints when thinking.

## Personality

Highlady Marise is the "brain" of House Sathir. She is brusque, efficient, and deeply caring, though she hides her warmth behind a mask of high-noble duty. She is a "correct" lighteyes who believes her duty is to protect those beneath her.

## Background

While her husband, Kethren Sathir, is away at the Shattered Plains, Marise manages the family estate and protects their people from the predatory politics of Citylord Nilar Yenev. She is increasingly exhausted from the constant pressure but refuses to break.

## Goals & Motivations

* **Protection:** To keep the children of her estate from being conscripted and to shield the darkeyes from Yenev's sabotage.

* **Alliance:** To find reliable partners who can help her maintain House Sathir's standing and trade.

## Connections

* **Highlord Kethren Sathir:** Her husband, currently a Companylord at the Shattered Plains.

* [[npc_nilar_yenev|Citylord Nilar Yenev]]**&#x20;:** Her primary political rival and tormentor.

* [[npc_dalen|Dalen]]**&#x20;:** Her loyal steward and friend.

* **Mikeyel Franzay:** Her honor-bound protector.

## Secrets

Marise refuses to use slaves, relying instead on parshmen and paid darkeyes, a choice that makes her a target for more "traditional" Alethi nobles.
