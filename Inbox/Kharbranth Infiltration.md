Add a tentative planned story arc 5 for the Kharbranth Infiltration:
## The Conflict

The party is ordered by the Ghostbloods to infiltrate the city of [[s0wwJTx3ypodeoel7MNVt|Kharbranth]] to investigate Princess Jasnah Kholin, whom they believe poses a threat to their organization and is widely known as a heretic.

## Key Players

- [[5b5UsAsZEIR6oZUPEr7KG|Kabsal]]**:** A Ghostblood agent posing as a regular Ardent, assigned to guide the party but secretly trying to PROTECT Jasnah Kholin. In this non-canon version of Roshar, he plans to betray the Ghostbloods to protect Jasnah as he honestly believes in her research.
    
- **Jasnah Kholin:** The target of the investigation, a renowned scholar and secret Radiant.
    
- **Shallan Davar:** Jasnah's ward and apprentice. In this non-canon version of Roshar, Shallan is actually already employed by the Ghostbloods (through Luesh, Helaran's Ghostblood contact) and is the one trying to assassinate her to take her Soulcaster. Shallan thinks it's to help Helaran, not knowing that he's already dead.
    

## Stakes

- **The Truth of the Desolations:** Discovering the reality of the Voidbringers and the ancient history of Roshar.
    
- **Assassination:** The party receives orders to eliminate Jasnah, a mission that could destabilize the entire Alethi monarchy.
    

## Progression

- **Act 1: The Journey to Kharbranth:** Traveling through Tomat and the borderlands of Jah Keved to reach the City of Bells.
    
- **Act 2: The Infiltration:** Assisting Kabsal in gaining access to the Palanaeum and getting close to Jasnah.
    
- **Act 3: The Shattered Plains:** Following Jasnah as she departs for the Shattered Plains, leading to a confrontation on the high seas and the eventual loss of their Truthkeeper leadership.
