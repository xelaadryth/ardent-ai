## Overview

A dangerous criminal organization based in Roshar and is starting to operate across planetary boundaries, the Bloodmarks have a main base in the city of Bodenar in Jah Keved, and established a foothold in the Horneater Peaks of Roshar for interstellar trade through the perpendicularity there.

## Goals & Agenda

Their most top secret operation involves the smuggling of gems and resources between Mythos and Roshar. They exploit the perpendicularity in the Horneater Peaks to transport Nurian gems, which may have unique properties or high value in the Rosharan economy. Generally they're involved heavily in:

- Firemoss sales
    
- Forgery - Selling false identification to a deceased lesser-house lighteyes in the far west (House Charash, annexed but paperwork was never filed)
    
- Smuggling darkwood, spheres, and other luxury items, upriver of Branchken they’ll take goods on foot by wagon to Leafken to cross the bridge to another towed barge just past Branchken - heavily guarded caravans with rough and foreign-looking mercenariesGhostbloods investigating smuggling up the windrunner river, influx of spheres has to be coming from somewhere
    
- Don’t help the Knights of Dusk; too risky to go against the city. Supplies Highlord Paladar with firemoss and smuggled luxury goods from Jah Keved and spheres to turn a blind eye
    
- Hasn’t helped the Truthkeepers yet but is willing to
    
- Debt collecting

They are ruthless and efficient, using intimidation and their knowledge of worldhopping to stay one step ahead of local authorities. They have earned the deep-seated grudge of N'tal.
