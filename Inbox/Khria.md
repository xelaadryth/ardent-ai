# Khria

## Appearance

Khria has the typical features of a Purelaker: lean, with a lithe build and skin tanned by long days in the sun and water. She often wears practical, muted clothing suitable for both travel and the urban shadows of Revolar. With her Truthwatcher powers, she often uses illusions to disguise herself as a short Alethi darkeyed woman.

## Personality

She is known for her preternatural calm and sharp logistical mind. In the heat of battle or a high-stakes meeting, Khria is the one calculating the best path forward. She is decisive and deeply loyal, though she carries a quiet bitterness toward the sister who "betrayed" her to the Truthkeepers.

## Background

A native of the Purelake, Khria's life changed when she spotted a Mistspren in the shallow waters. Her sister mentioned the "strange fish" to other townsfolk, who—fearing for her soul—reported her to the Truthkeepers. She was seized and scheduled for execution before the Knights of Dusk struck the caravan. She is more than a lieutenant; she is a founder of the rebellion, dedicated to bringing back the Radiants.

## Goals & Motivations

Khria wants to see the people of Vamah freed from oppression, but her deepest motivation is finding a way to safely reunite with or protect her brother, despite the circumstances of her capture.
