# Tien
## Appearance

Tien is a young man with a bright, earnest face and curly hair often dusty from the front lines. He lacks the physical imposingness of his late brother, Kaladin, but there is a resilient light in his eyes.

## Personality

Tien is naturally artistic and empathetic, often finding beauty in small things like interesting rocks or the way the light hits the stormwall. He is currently struggling with immense survivor's guilt, feeling that he is "living on borrowed time" bought by his brother's blood. However he is possessed with strong determination to improve himself to not need other's protection, training the hardest out of everyone and rapidly becoming a force to be reckoned with, although short of the one-man army Kaladin would have become.

## Background

After Kaladin died saving him in the Jah Keved border skirmishes, Tien threw himself into the military to honor his brother's sacrifice. He is a competent soldier but lacks the "killer instinct," often finding himself being protected by his squadmates—many of whom have died to keep him safe.

## Goals & Motivations

He wants to be worthy of the lives spent to keep him alive. He currently fights for the Sadeas Princedom, unaware that his destiny involves becoming a Windrunner and a key figure in a world-saving prophecy.

## Connections

- **Kaladin:** His fallen brother and personal hero.
    
- **The Players:** Destiny will soon pull them into his orbit.
    
- **The Windrunners:** He is the candidate meant to lead them in this timeline.
