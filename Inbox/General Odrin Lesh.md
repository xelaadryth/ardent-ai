# General Odrin Lesh
## Appearance

A man of sharp features and a polished, military bearing. He wears an embellished brown and gray of Highlord Paladar's favored commanders, often carrying a ceremonial blade that he is more than capable of using.

## Personality

Ambitious and ruthless. Odrin Lesh views the world as a game of territory and influence. He has little regard for the common folk of Revolar, seeing them only as resources to be managed or suppressed.

## Background

A close friend of Highlord Paladar, Odrin Lesh was installed as General of Revolar after [[npc_general_spade|Antisar Spadis]] was ousted. He is a primary architect of the region's current instability, secretly funding bandits in Sadeas lands to justify Paladar's "protection" and the northern expansion of the Vamah borders.

## Goals & Motivations

- **Expansion:** To increase Paladar's territory (and his own influence) by destabilizing neighboring princedoms.
    
- **Suppression:** To completely dismantle the Knights of Dusk and any remaining loyalty to Spadis.
    

## Connections

- **Highlord Paladar:** His patron and co-conspirator.
    
- [[npc_general_spade|General Spade]] **:** His predecessor and bitter rival.
    
- **The Truthkeepers:** His primary allies in maintaining order and hunting "insurgents."
