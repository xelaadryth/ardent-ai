# Grand Justicar Malice
## Appearance

A man of imposing presence, Malice wears the ornate, heavy robes of a religious fanatic—deep violets and golds that signal his high station. He has the classic Veden features of red hair and pale skin, though his eyes are a cold, light grey. He is rarely seen without his staff of office, which is capped with a suspiciously large and perfectly cut emerald.

## Personality

Malice is the embodiment of "the law as a weapon." He is cold, clinical, and possesses a terrifyingly sharp intellect. He views people as assets or obstacles and has no qualms about using extreme violence to maintain his version of order. Unlike many on Roshar, he possesses a cosmic perspective, making him patient and exceptionally dangerous.

## Background

One of the four Grand Justicars of the [[RPZ-tfzIqzRhfboC6cgfN|Bloodmarks]], Malice has spent decades consolidating power in [[JlRniRHuHoKcG8CVD1tbZ|Bodenar]]. While his peers focus on local squabbles, Malice discovered the existence of a Perpendicularity and the secrets of the Cognitive Realm. He used this knowledge to fuel the growth of the [[RPZ-tfzIqzRhfboC6cgfN|Bloodmarks]], using them as his personal shadow-army. It was Malice who orchestrated the sabotage of the [[ZOWhgc_9NJz8OMvKu3X6o|Ghostbloods]] gem deal, ordering the ambush that resulted in the death of [[Sbddxxcp_K83M9wuLSnYd|N'tal]]'s family to secure control over the [[kA7mYskuyJPSTksWkIdEY|Nurian gem]] trade.

## Goals & Motivations

- **Monopoly on Worldhopping:** He seeks to be the sole gatekeeper of the Perpendicularity in the Horneater Peaks, giving him leverage over both the Alethi and extraterrestrial interests.
    
- **Nurian Dominance:** He wants to ensure the [[RPZ-tfzIqzRhfboC6cgfN|Bloodmarks]] control the supply and application of [[kA7mYskuyJPSTksWkIdEY|Nurian gems]], viewing the experimental steamship as a tool for his own expansion.
    
- **Elimination of the Ghostbloods:** He views the Ghostbloods as his primary rivals and seeks to systematically dismantle their influence in Bodenar.
    

## Connections

- **Arbiter Jalis**: One of his ruthless field agents, whom he sent to carry out the ambush on [[Sbddxxcp_K83M9wuLSnYd|N'tal]]'s family. (See [[mPx1yhWISBaUtSI1Aup1j|The Foundry Gambit]])
    
- [[Sbddxxcp_K83M9wuLSnYd|N'tal]]: The survivor he missed. Malice is aware that someone survived the ambush and is wary of the loose thread.
    
- [[ZOWhgc_9NJz8OMvKu3X6o|Ghostbloods]]: Deeply resentful for killing their agents and ruining their gem deal.
    

## Secrets

Malice is currently the only leader in the [[RPZ-tfzIqzRhfboC6cgfN|Bloodmarks]] who knows about the Perpendicularity and the ability to travel through the Cognitive Realm. He has been using this "hidden path" to move high-value goods and assassins without being detected.
