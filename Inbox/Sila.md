# Sila
## Appearance

A large, imposing darkeyed man who looks even more dangerous than he is. He is marked by numerous battle scars, most notably a jagged diagonal gash that crosses his lips. He carries a massive, well-used axe and typically smells of smoke and cheap ale.

## Personality

Sila is craftier than he looks. While he presents as a simple thug, he is a survivor who isn't "sold" on the Truthkeepers' ideology or any higher cause. He likes drink, and he tends to waste his money as soon as he earns it—which keeps him constantly looking for the next "quick buck."

## Background

Originally a low-tier darkeyes at the Truthkeeper camp, Sila was hired (for a single ruby broam) to stop [[npc_doug|Doug]] (Guddy) from reaching Salimset. After the attempt failed and he was sentenced to hard labor, he was liberated by [[npc_chip_of_the_ghostbloods|Chip]] and [[npc_karani|Karani]]. To escape the Truthkeepers' justice, he faked his own death in a fire accident. He has since fled south to [[location_revolar|Revolar]] to work for the Ghostbloods as a freelancer.

## Goals & Motivations

- **Survival:** His primary goal is to avoid the notice of the Truthkeepers, who believe him to be dead.
    
- **Wealth:** He is always looking for his next payout, though it rarely stays in his pockets for long.
    
- **Service to the Ghostbloods:** Having been saved by them, he is now a loyal (if expensive) asset for their operations in Revolar.
    

## Connections

- [[npc_chip_of_the_ghostbloods|Chip]] **:** One of his saviors and a current contact within the Ghostbloods.
    
- [[npc_karani|Karani]] **:** The other half of the team that freed him from hard labor.
    
- [[la_truthkeepers|Truthkeepers]] **:** His former "employers" whom he must now avoid at all costs.
    

## Secrets

Sila's "death" by fire was meticulously staged with a burned skeleton and everything. If any [[la-VYCfiPyoH3437byMpk|Truthkeepers]] (like [[notHWuxSFgK2rUi6e4y8P|Adjudicator Peton]]) were to recognize him in [[FeF1W6cnNv-k8ME9pfXIV|Revolar]] , the ruse would be up.
