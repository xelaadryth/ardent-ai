# Undertaker
## Appearance

Undertaker is a man of indeterminate age with a weathered, yet sophisticated face. He often wears high-quality, though understated, Alethi silks in dark, somber tones. He carries himself with the quiet confidence of a predator and is rarely seen without a small, exotic avian (just called a chicken, like all other birds on Roshar) perched nearby. He often smells faintly of strange perfumes and dried herbs. His eyebrows are dyed black and cut short, hiding his Thaylen origins except to the most discerning eyes.

## Personality

Calculating, patient, and intensely pragmatic. He speaks with a soft, melodic voice with almost no hint of Thaylen accent that carries an underlying threat. He views the world as a grand game of logistics and influence. He is not a zealot; he cares nothing for the Truthkeepers' ideology, but he admires their efficiency in maintaining "order." He is a collector of curiosities and information, valuing anything that grants leverage.

## Background

Operating out of the shadows of [[location_revolar|Revolar]], Undertaker rarely walks the streets without a fresh disguise. Sometimes a weathered soldier, sometimes a merchant, in reality, he is a high-ranking member of the [[faction_ghostbloods|Ghostbloods]], overseeing their operations in the region. He reports to Iyatil, who pretends to be his apprentice.

## Goals & Motivations

- **Regional Stability:** He wants to keep the peace at all costs so that trade and research into fabrials and worldhopping can continue unhindered.
    
- **Suppression of the Past:** He actively works to bury information regarding the Voidbringers or the true history of Roshar, believing such revelations would shatter the social order he exploits.
    
- **Managing the Radiant Threat:** He views the emergence of "Possessed" (Radiants) as a wild card that threatens stability. He supports the [[faction_truthkeepers|Truthkeepers]] and [[faction_silent_oath|Silent Oath]] in pruning these elements, unless they can be brought under his specific, covert control.
    

## Connections

- [[faction_ghostbloods|Ghostbloods]]: His true allegiance.
    
- [[npc_chip|Chip]] **&** [[npc_karani|Karani]]: His field agents.
    
- [[npc_high_enforcer_aylen|High Enforcer Aylen]]: He views Aylen's work with the Silent Oath as a necessary "cleaning service" for the world at large.
    

## Secrets

Undertaker is actually **Mraize**, a worldhopper of significant power and influence. He is not just a merchant; he is a hunter of the highest caliber. He knows far more about the "Possessed" than he lets on and is currently looking for a way to bind Radiants to the Ghostbloods' service as a permanent, controllable asset.
