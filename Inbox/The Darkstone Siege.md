# The Darkstone Siege
## Setup

Outmatched and pursued by a large war-band of the [[UosLnowxcsUZ7qi819lHO|Knights of Dusk]] within the [[5MCm_03NYayD3q3F2skrz|Darkstone Forest]], the party has taken refuge in a sturdy, abandoned stone lodge. They have sent an emergency spanreed message for help, but they must hold the line until reinforcements arrive.

## Environment

- **The Stone Lodge:** A two-story structure with thick walls but several windows and a weak rear door.
    
- **Defensive Prep:** The party has 2 rounds to barricade doors, move furniture, or set traps before the first wave hits.
    
- **Reinforcement Timer:** Reinforcements from the [[la-VYCfiPyoH3437byMpk|Truthkeepers]] or [[pWv26vpnMbTxOivgXu5nz|Silent Oath]] will arrive in 5 rounds.
    

## Enemies/NPCs

- **Wave 1:** 4 Skirmishers trying to force the windows.
    
- **Wave 2:** 2 Heavies with a ram targeting the front door, supported by 2 Archers in the treeline.
    
- **Wave 3:** A "Dusk Caller" (Officer) who attempts to negotiate a surrender before the final push.
    

## Tactics

The Knights of Dusk use the forest's gloom for cover, darting in to test the defenses. Every round starting Round 3, [[iTlwIxIaRO1BfXrSRT-rm|General Spade]] cuts a new entrance in the walls. In Round 4, [[yV_he7KROzW7kW8AK1uPx|Khria]] enters with a Truthkeeper force, but the spanreed says that reinforcements are almost there.

## Resolution

- **Success:** The party holds out. The sound of Truthkeeper horns signals the arrival of [[notHWuxSFgK2rUi6e4y8P|Adjudicator Peton]] or even a unit of the [[pWv26vpnMbTxOivgXu5nz|Silent Oath]], scattering the rebels.
    
- **Failure:** The building is breached or burned. The party is captured and taken to a Knights of Dusk hideout for interrogation.
