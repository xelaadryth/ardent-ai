# Citylord Nilar Yenev

## Appearance

Pale violet eyes that are almost clear. He is in his late 20s and carries himself with a quiet, too-calm demeanor. He is the classic "bad" lighteyes—polished, reasonable-sounding, and utterly ruthless.

## Personality

Nilar Yenev is a political predator who specializes in removing sources of risk and furthering Vamah causes at any cost. He speaks quietly and sounds reasonable even when lying smoothly. He treats people as hostages or tools, liberally using and mistreating slaves.

## Background

The son of a Veden family that moved east, Nilar was politically maneuvered into power in Stringken. He works tirelessly to wrest control and credibility away from Highlord Sathir, using blackmail and sabotage to make himself look good at the expense of others.

## Goals & Motivations

* **Suppression:** To move all able-bodied men west to the Veden border skirmishes, weakening the local labor force.

* **Credit Theft:** He plans to "lose" gumfrem gemhearts (taxes) on purpose to embarrass Sathir, then magically "recover" them himself to take the credit.

* **Assassination:** He wants to assassinate the party and seize their gemhearts.

He is willing to sabotage his own princedom's taxes just to secure a political victory over House Sathir.
