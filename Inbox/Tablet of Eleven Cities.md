## Summary

An ancient, weather-worn stone wall carving found within a forgotten temple dedicated to the Radiant orders.

## Details

The tablet depicts the ten legendary Dawncities, but it features a significant damage-mark or hidden engraving that points to a "secret 11th city" named Urithiru. The text suggests that this city is currently locked or hidden away from the world.

## Implications

Discovery of this tablet is what triggered the memories of the prophecy in Dreamwaker. It provides the players with a concrete goal: find the location of Urithiru.

## What People Believe

The existence of an 11th Dawncity is considered a myth or a theological curiosity by most modern scholars of Roshar. The Truthkeepers, despite their name, do not seem to have this information in their current archives.

# ## The Ten Silver Kingdoms

### 1. Aimia

- **Dawncity:** Akinah (covered with a fake Oathgate)
    
- **Inhabitants:** Home to the **Siah Aimians** (resistant to soulcasting, shadows cast toward light, deep blue crystalline eyes) and the **Dysian Aimians** (Sleepless, composed of swarms of hordelings).
    

### 2. Alethela (Modern Alethkar)

- **Dawncity:** Kholinar
    
- **Role:** Historically dedicated to the arts of war and protection, standing as the bulwark against the Voidbringers.
    

### 3. Iri

- **Dawncity:** Rall Elorim (The City of Shadows)
    
- **Culture:** Iriali have golden hair and metallic bronze skin. They worship **The One**, believing all people are fragments of a single being experiencing the universe. Rira was once part of this kingdom but has since separated.
    

### 4. Natanatan

- **Dawncity:** Narak (Stormseat), now the [[w5nWclD3pE_P2atdZiIqA|Shattered Plains]]
    
- **Culture:** The Natan people have pale bluish skin and wool-like white hair, traditionally believed to be the descendants of the second moon, **Nomon**.
    

### 5. Makabakam

- **Dawncities:** Azimir (Capital of Azir), NOT Sesemalex Dar (Capital of Emul)
    
- **Culture:** A vast region now split into many nations (Azir, Emul, Tashikk, etc.). In Tashikk, the Herald [[eHXnOG1j49L2zlPNEP5Xe|Ishar]] is worshipped as **Tashi**, the Binder of Gods and leader of the Heralds.
    

### 6. Rishir

- **Dawncity:** Kurth
    
- **Region:** Includes the Reshi Isles and the mainland portions now known as northern Alethkar, Jah Keved, and Herdaz.
    

### 7. Sela Tales

- **Dawncity:** Panatham
    
- **Culture:** Encompasses the nations of the Purelake border (Yulay, Marabethia). Ethnically Selay.
    

### 8. Shin Kak Nish (Shinovar)

- **Dawncity:** Unknown
    
- **Geography:** The only region of Roshar with soil and traditional "chicken" birds, protected by the Mists of the Highstorms.
    

### 9. Thalath

- **Dawncity:** Thaylen City
    
- **Geography:** Once spanning a vast portion of the south (including [[s0wwJTx3ypodeoel7MNVt|Kharbranth]] and the Frostlands), it is now primarily reduced to the island of Thaylenah.
    

### 10. Valhav (Modern Jah Keved)

- **Dawncity:** Vedenar
    
- **Culture:** The ancestral home of the Veden people, known for their red hair and fierce internal politics.

## The Dawncities

The Dawncities are the oldest continuously inhabited locations on Roshar. Their origins are attributed to the Heralds or the Almighty Himself. The 8 containing Oathgates (2 unknown):

- **Akinah:** Lost to the mists of Aimia; rumored to house ancient secrets.
    
- **Azimir:** Famous for its intricate bronze architecture.
    
- **Kholinar:** Built into the wind-shadow of a massive rock formation; home to the Windrunning Aegis.
    
- **Kurth:** The capital of Rira, located in the Silver Kingdom of Rishir at the time (instead of present day Iri)
    
- **Narak:** Destroyed in the past; its remains now form the center of the Shattered Plains.
    
- **Panatham:** Located in Babatharnam, the coastal center of Sela Tales.
    
- **Rall Elorim:** Known as the "City of Shadows" due to its unique lighting and Iriali culture.
    
- **Thaylen City:** Built with massive "Longwalls" to withstand the strongest Highstorms.
    
- **Vedenar:** The fortified heart of the Veden people.
    

### False Dawncity

- **Sesemalex Dar:** A city carved into the very stone, featuring immense canyons and plateaus.
