## Districts

- Around City
    
    - Bendfields - Grazing for ranched animals hunting retracting tall grass to the north, largely untended
        
    - Shellfarms - Covered areas to the west of the city, better stormbreak, ranching
        
    - Cremfall Rise - Heavy deposits of crem to the east that grow and shrink with the storms, rock formations
        
    - [[5MCm_03NYayD3q3F2skrz|Darkstone Forest]] - Darkwood trees encased in crem to the east of Cremfall Rise towards the river
        
- Outer Districts
    
    - Scarstone Quarter - East part of the city, partially in the Cremfall. Weak ground and unstable foundations, takes the brunt of the storms, in need of repair
        
        - Barrel Row
            
        - Crowded Steppe
            
        - Lowstone Alley
            
        - Bruckslats - Ghostblood hideout
            
        - [[hUQ6_yOrKck133aphc6I-|The Stackhouses]]
            
        - Cremling Quarter
            
    - Slipmarket - Docks to the southeast of the city, near the south gate caravans and trade ships, stalls that fold down and stow away. Pops up massive like the fields of tall grass north of the city. Shipments mainly come from the mountains to the southwest
        
    - The Driftwards - travelers, outsiders, transient populations, rougher district near the north gate
        
        - Bladeshine Inn, The Crossed Paths, Gloam Lantern, Stormrest, Greenplank Inn, [[0ocOkbVp8y480cE8HkOph|Cream of the Crem]], [[DiROO4huf23PFVHsj-y9A|Whitespine's Fang]] - Sweeper’s inn to meet N’tal
            
    - Devotary Ward - towards the southwest “If you’re looking for answers, try the Ward. If you’re looking for results, try the Guard.”
        
        - Veristitalians (vuhristaTAHLians) - not part of a Vorin devotary
            
        - Sincerity (learning, Book of Endless Pages allows for questioning of Vorin tenets)
            
        - Purity (honesty, religion, natural history)
            
        - Insight (study texts)
            
        - Denial (asceticism)
            
        - Mind (history, linguistics, literature)
            
        - Mercy (care for troubled and helpless)
            
        - Kalak (judgments/decisions)
            
        - Order of Talenelat (commonfolk, dislike theological debate)
            
        - Scholar’s Rest, Candle Quarter
            
    - FIeldwards - to the west, agricultural, farming, tanneries, grain processing, warehouses
