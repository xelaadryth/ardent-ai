# Highlord Paladar

## Appearance

Tall and bald with striking, bright yellow eyes. He is in his late 40s and carries himself with the refined, arrogant grace of a high-ranking noble. He speaks with a crisp, dramatic accent.

## Personality

Greedy, corrupt, and remarkably harsh. Paladar is a man of grand gestures and dramatic blinks, hiding a ruthless political mind. He is focused entirely on the expansion of his own influence and the territory of the Vamah Princedom.

## Background

The reigning Regent of Vamah, Paladar has exploited the absence of other high-ranking nobles (who are at the Shattered Plains) to seize control of the region. He ousted [[iTlwIxIaRO1BfXrSRT-rm|General Spade]] in favor of his friend Odrin Lesh and is currently engaged in a bitter feud with House Sathir.

## Goals & Motivations

- **Territorial Expansion:** To move the Vamah borders north into Sadeas lands through sabotage and "protection."
    
- **Support for Militants:** He secretly supplies militant groups in southeast Sadeas to maintain a state of unrest that justifies his rule.
    

## Connections

- [[npc_general_odrin_lesh|General Odrin Lesh]] **:** His close friend and military enforcer.
    
- [[npc_chip_of_the_ghostbloods|Chip]] **:** His third son, Archisal Paladar, though their relationship is strained.
    
- **Citylord Nilar Yenev:** A political ally and subordinate in the Veden-Alethi borderlands.
    

## Secrets

Paladar is using the "Possessed" as a political tool, allowing the Truthkeepers to purge his enemies while he profits from the chaos.
