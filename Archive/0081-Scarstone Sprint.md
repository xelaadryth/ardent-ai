# Scarstone Sprint
## Setup

The party is in high-speed pursuit of a high-value target (likely a [[UosLnowxcsUZ7qi819lHO|Knights of Dusk]] agent) through the winding, industrial alleys of the **Scarstone Quarter**. The target’s allies are positioned to delay the party at every turn.

## Environment

- **The Scarstone Quarter:** A maze of narrow alleys, rickety scaffolding, and buckets of slick cremwater within [[FeF1W6cnNv-k8ME9pfXIV|Revolar]].
    
- **Dynamic Obstacles:**
    
    - **Locked Gates:** Heavy iron grates that require a Strength or Thievery check to bypass quickly.
        
    - **Scaffold Collapse:** Archers fire at support beams to drop crates and timber into the party's path.
        
    - **Slick Cremwater:** Narrow passages that fill with slick cremwater, requiring timing or an Agility test to pass without slowing down.
        

## Enemies/NPCs

- **Dusk Archers (4):** Positioned on rooftops. Their goal isn't necessarily to kill, but to pin the party down or trigger environmental hazards.
    
- **Dusk Saboteurs (2):** Armed with axes and nets to create physical barriers.
    
- **The Target:** A fleet-footed scout moving three zones ahead of the party.
    

## Tactics

The enemies focus on "The Delay." If the party spends more than 5 rounds reaching the final warehouse, the target escapes through a secret hatch, and the party is surrounded by a dozen arriving Dusk reinforcements (a "Fight or Flight" situation).

## Resolution

- **Success:** The party captures the target or corners them before reinforcements arrive, gaining intelligence on the [[5MCm_03NYayD3q3F2skrz|Darkstone Forest]] operations.
    
- **Failure:** The target escapes, and the party must flee the Scarstone Quarter to avoid being overwhelmed by the insurgent numbers.
