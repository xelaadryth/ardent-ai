If the players remain loyal, they will be complicit in the execution of a just hero (Spade). If they turn, they will be hunted by the [[la-VYCfiPyoH3437byMpk|Truthkeepers]] and the terrifyingly powerful [[pWv26vpnMbTxOivgXu5nz|Silent Oath]].
