Add a faction called the Envisagers:
## Summary

A radical and secretive death cult in a rural area of Alethkar, in the village of Talinan. They believe that by placing themselves or their children in life-threatening situations, they can force the return of the legendary Knights Radiant. They originally lived in a village called Talinar, which was razed by Truthkeepers and covered up as the actions of a Possessed.

## Details

- **Beliefs:** They hold that the "Lost Radiants" will only return to those who demonstrate extreme resilience through hardship. This leads to abusive practices, including child neglect and "trials" that often end in death.
    
- **The "Great Intervention":** They look for signs of divine intervention by the Almighty or the Radiants during these trials.
    
- **Connection to Radiants:** Unlike most Rosharans who view the Radiants as traitors (the Lost Radiants), the Envisagers view them as the only salvation against the coming darkness.
    

## Implications

The group is dangerous not because of military might, but because of their fanatical devotion. They often hide in plain sight in small farming communities. The Truthkeepers view them as a "contagion" or a source of "Possessed" behavior.

## What People Believe

Most Alethi consider them "storming insane" or dangerous heretics. The Truthkeepers, under High Enforcer Aylen, have been known to wipe out entire Envisager villages to "prevent the contagion from spreading."
