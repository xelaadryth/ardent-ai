# The Cinder Evacuation
## Scene Goal

To rescue survivors from a sudden fire and uncover a suspicious conspiracy involving pre-planned arson at a noble estate.

## Opening

As the party enters the noble district of [[FeF1W6cnNv-k8ME9pfXIV|Revolar]], they see a massive plume of black smoke. The [[0YaZuf8_UJeWQ0pZv51rV|Highlord Paladar]]-aligned estate is engulfed in flames. Screams come from the lower servants' quarters, where wreckage has pinned several workers.

## Key Beats

- **The Rescue:** The party must enter the burning structure. Use a "Clock" or rounds to represent the spreading fire. They find three servants pinned under a collapsed marble beam in the kitchens.
    
- **The "Professional" Response:** Almost immediately, a highly efficient team of water-bearers and artifabrians arrive outside. They use specialized fabrials to quarantine the fire with unnatural speed, preventing it from spreading to adjacent buildings.
    
- **The Suspicion:** As the servants are pulled out, they are terrified—not of the fire, but of being "late." One mutters, "We were supposed to be out by the second bell... the delay, it wasn't our fault!"
    

## Branches

- **Investigation:** If the party questions the servants or the "emergency responders," they find the responders are actually on the payroll of the [[UosLnowxcsUZ7qi819lHO|Knights of Dusk]], acting with suspicious foresight.
    
- **Sentimental Value:** While transporting personal possessions, a children's book with high sentimental value to one of [[0YaZuf8_UJeWQ0pZv51rV|Highlord Paladar]]'s sons ([[AsV00GwhEas4A0eEBDA3k|Chip]]) was unaccounted for, and the servants had gone back to search for it, thinking the reason they were to evacuate was just for a cremling extermination crew that they were supposed to keep on the down low.
    

## Transition

The fire is contained in minutes, leaving only the noble's main manor a smoking ruin. The local guards move in quickly to shoo away witnesses, leaving the party with more questions than answers about who exactly wanted this building gone. Secretly though the Knights of Dusk are targeting corrupt nobles to hurt their image and resources to distract them from resisting being overthrown. While the Knights of Dusk think of themselves as freedom fighters and the good guys, they are constantly referred to as terrorists by the current leaders of Revolar.
