# Morrow
## Appearance (Deceased)

Morrow is a man who looks older than his years, with a tangled beard and eyes that dart frantically as if watching something invisible in the air. He wears the travel-stained clothes of a merchant, though they are now little more than rags.

## Personality

Confused and terrified, he often speaks in half-finished sentences or mutters to the "shadows" following him. He is inherently kind, often trying to share his meager scraps of food with others, even while in a state of distress. He is driven by a desire to do good to atone for a shadowy past.

## Background

Once a merchant who found himself embroiled in dark dealings, Morrow turned his life around to become an honest man. However, his "snapping" occurred recently, and he began to see strange, geometric figures (Cryptics) that seem to whisper truths about his past.

## Goals & Motivations

He wants to find peace and to do enough good in the world to outweigh the secrets he carries—secrets the Cryptics find fascinating.
