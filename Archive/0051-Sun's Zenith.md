# Sun's Zenith

**Sun's Zenith** • High-End Inn & Tavern • Gilden District, Revolar • Front for the [[RPZ-tfzIqzRhfboC6cgfN|Bloodmarks]]

- **Aspects**: Polished brass and heavy mahogany | Perpetual "No Vacancy" sign at the desk | Discreet, well-dressed "merchants" occupying every corner table
    
- **First Impression**: A stately three-story stone building with a gleaming sun embossed in brass over the entrance. Inside, the air smells of expensive sandalwood and fine wine. The lighting is warm and bright, provided by high-grade spheres in crystal lanterns. Soft conversation and the clink of quality silverware create a veneer of professional prosperity.
    
- **Mood**: Exclusive, Professional, Vigilant
    

## Overview

The **Sun's Zenith** is an upscale inn in the Gilden District that serves as the premier logistical hub for the [[RPZ-tfzIqzRhfboC6cgfN|Bloodmarks]] criminal syndicate. To avoid prying eyes and unexpected guests, the establishment is perpetually "fully booked" by long-term "scholars" and "wealthy merchants"—who are, in reality, Bloodmark agents and guards. While it maintains an impeccable public reputation, its true purpose remains the high-level processing of smuggled gems.

## Atmosphere & Layout

The inn is designed for privacy and security. The stone walls are thick enough to muffle conversations, and the seating is arranged in high-backed booths that shield patrons from view.

- **The Common Room**: The main floor is an exercise in elegance, featuring velvet-lined booths and a bar of dark, polished wood. The "patrons" here are mostly well-dressed syndicate enforcers acting as a quiet, professional security force.
    
- **The Back Office ("The Vault")**: Accessible only through a hidden door behind the bar, this reinforced room is where the real high-level meetings take place.
    
- **The Cellars**: Used for storing standard ale, but contains several false-bottomed barrels used to transport contraband.
    

## Interactive Elements

- **Nomos (Barkeep)**: The primary contact for the syndicate. He presents as a consummate professional, offering impeccable service while silently gauging every newcomer's worth. See **Nomos** for details.
    
- **Social Infiltration**: The Bloodmarks are currently expecting the arrival of **Guddy**, a former [[ZOWhgc_9NJz8OMvKu3X6o|Ghostblood]] traitor.
    
    - **Pretending to be Guddy**: If the party approaches Nomos claiming to be Guddy and his crew, they must provide the correct codewords.
        
    - **The Codeword**: _"Ask the second moon for the suns."_
        
    - **The Reception**: If successful, Nomos will usher them into the back to "finalize the paperwork." However, the Bloodmarks do not fully trust Guddy (or those pretending to be him) and will be watching for any sign of a double-cross.
        
- **Firemoss Trade**: Small amounts of firemoss can be purchased here by those who know the right signs, though Nomos prefers to keep the "heavy business" in the back.
    

## History & Purpose

Originally a failing merchant's rest, the Sun's Zenith was "purchased" (likely through debt intimidation) by the Bloodmarks three years ago. It now serves as the "Brightest Inn" referenced in intercepted communications, serving as the transition point for those defecting from other organizations—most notably the upcoming "initiation" of the traitor Guddy.
