# Kuma

## Appearance

Kuma stands 5'6" and possesses a mix of Shin and Alethi features, allowing him to pass as either depending on his dress. He has dark blue eyes (darkeyed) and thick black hair.

## Personality

Kuma is defined by an obsessive drive to create and repair. Whether it's a mundane piece of farm equipment or a legendary Soulcaster, he cannot help but try to improve it. He is a "maker" in every sense of the word, often acting on a creative instinct that his spren, Viscose, finds frustratingly illogical.

## Background

Born to a Nurian family with a farming background but neglected as a child, Kuma was always more interested in books and tinkering with the tools of the trade than the harvest itself. He was raised by his uncle, who eventually gave him a mysterious device with a large gemstone—a gift from his parents that had never worked until Kuma infused it with Stormlight. He is now traveling to Revolar to find the Artifabrians and uncover the truth of his parents' whereabouts.

Unknown to him, his parents were forced to work for them in Thaylenah on their questionable fabrial research for torture devices, but since they didn't want to worry Kuma or the uncle, they left them in the dark.

## Goals

* **Find Parents:** Discover where his parents went and why they left him with his uncle.

* **Legendary Crafting:** To create items of mythic power, surpassing even the ancient fabrials.

## Connections

* [[npc_viscose|Viscose]] His logical and contrarian Inkspren.

* **The Artifabrians:** A group in Revolar he intends to seek out.

* **Uncle:** The man who raised him and nearly lost his inheritance in a card game.

## Mechanics (Elsecaller)

Kuma will become an **Elsecaller**:

* **Transportation:** He can see the "souls" of people, perceive emotionspren, sense Investiture, and even see through walls by peering into the Cognitive Realm.

* **Transformation (Soulcasting):** He has an intuitive sense for materials; metals seem to shift into what he needs, and he often finds he has more crafting supplies on hand than he realized.

* **The Soulcaster:** He possesses a damaged Soulcaster that he believers has begun to function again due to his recharge and repairs, but is actually because he is unknowingly using Elsecaller abilities.

# Mallow

## Appearance

Dark green eyes and black hair. She is a Purelaker (Tu Bayla) and often wears practical traveling gear suited for a life on the move, though she still feels most at home in the simple clothes of a fisher.

## Personality

Mallow carries an anxious protectiveness over her animal companion. While she initially claimed to only want to return to the Purelake, she has discovered a surprising wonder in the wider world of Roshar. She is driven by intense guilt over her role in her sister's disappearance.

## Background

Mallow was raised by her overprotective sister, Khria, after their parents passed away. Her world shattered when Khria found a "strange fish" (a spren) that seemed to drive her mad. In a moment of panic and superstition, Mallow reported the incident, leading the Truthkeepers to take Khria away. Guilt-ridden, Mallow tracked the Truthkeepers across Roshar, eventually joining them as an "Enforcer" to find a way to save her sister.

## Goals

* **Save Khria:** Find her sister and rescue her from the Truthkeepers or her own madness.

* **Seek Atonement:** Face the betrayal she committed against her sister.

* **Protect the Forgotten:** She feels a natural kinship with orphans, the poor, and the hungry.

## Connections

* [[npc_khria|Khria]]**:** Her sister, now a lieutenant in the Knights of Dusk and a Truthwatcher.

* [[npc_cadence|Cadence]]**:** Her moody and empathetic Lightspren (also known as a reacher).

* Pom: Her loyal mink companion, almost injured by her original owner Khria when she was "driven mad".

## Mechanics (Willshaper)

Mallow will be a **Willshaper**. She will have access to the surges of **Transportation** (allowing her to enter the Cognitive Realm or "blink" through space) and **Cohesion** (altering the shape of solid objects). She is a seeker of freedom, both for herself and others.

# Galeth-son-Thald

## Background: The Weight of Command

Galeth-son-Thald was once a leader of men within the borders of Shinovar. That life ended the moment a soldier under his direct command took the life of an innocent girl during a chaotic skirmish. In the eyes of the Shin, where to lead is to be "lower" than those who work the land, this failure was absolute. As one who *subtracts*—a warrior—he believes his soul is already forfeit.

He did not flee to the Stone Lands for adventure; he fled as an act of self-exile. He chose the East specifically to be among the Alethi, a people he views as a reflection of his own nature: killers who embrace the blade. By surrounding himself with war and "fellow monsters," he lives out a permanent penance, believing that this grim, violent existence is all he is worth.

### Personality & Beliefs

* **The Broken Taboo:** Unlike his kin, Galeth has abandoned the reverence for stone. He walks upon it not because he is brave, but because he feels he is no longer worthy of the "holy" soil of Shinovar. The cold hardness of stone matches the state of his heart.

* **Grim Fatalism:** He carries a "this is all I'm worth" attitude. He takes the most dangerous assignments and the most grueling tasks for the party, viewing his survival almost as a continued punishment.

* **The Quiet Shin:** He remains soft-spoken and humble, but it lacks the traditional Shin politeness. It is the silence of a man who doesn't believe his voice has any right to be heard.

### Connections

* **The Truthkeepers:** He sees them as a necessary tool—a place where a killer can be pointed at "possessed" threats to perhaps do one last bit of good before he falls.

* **The Alethi:** He views them with a dark kinship. He doesn't admire their "glory" in battle; he recognizes their shared blood-stained hands.

# Fina

## Appearance

Light brown eyes and light brown hair. Fina stands 5'7" and carries herself with the poise of an Alethi noble, though her hands are often stained with ink, herbs, or chemicals from her medical and occult studies.

## Personality

Fina is a complex mix of high-society grace and pragmatic ruthlessness. While she struggles with people-pleasing and the guilt of her familial obligations, she is cold and efficient when it comes to protecting her friends, showing no moral qualms about lying, betraying, or even killing if it serves her long-term goals. She is intensely curious about the "spirit realm" and the scientific side of the occult.

## Background

Born into a noble family of knights, Fina was expected to marry well and uphold her house's honor. Instead, she became obsessed with medicine, poisons, and "lab records." After her family tried to marry her off to a "weird nature cult," she fled through the Cognitive Realm to find a place where she could define her own destiny.

## Goals

* **The Secluded Lab:** Establish a private place to conduct her experiments.

* **The Encyclopedia:** Catalog every species of flora and fauna on Roshar.

* **Genuine Acceptance:** Find friends who support her choices rather than her status.

* **Spirit Realm Secrets:** Uncover the truth behind the deepest layers of the Cosmere.

## Connections

* **[[npc_verdae|Verdae]]**:** Her naive and vine-like Cultivationspren.

## Mechanics (Edgedancer)

Fina will become an **Edgedancer** with access to **Abrasion** (slickness/friction control) and **Progression** (healing and regrowth).

* **Medical Expertise:** She combines her surges with a deep knowledge of surgery, medicine, and poisons.

* **The Forgotten:** Her oaths push her to remember the weak, the injured, and the forgotten—a stark contrast to her willingness to eliminate those she deems a threat.

* **Weapon:** Short bow.

# Iiko

## Appearance

Iiko has heterochromatic eyes—one dark brown and one light yellow—and stands 5'6" with an Alethi build, though they are originally from Nuria on Mythos. They rarely stand still, their disciplined monastic posture frequently collapsing into animated gestures or distracted wandering.

## Personality

Iiko is anything but the "quiet seeker." They are a whirlwind of energy—lively, eccentric, and often completely oblivious to social cues or physical danger. They seek fundamental truths with a manic intensity, seeing the world through a "Veristitalian" lens that is often distorted by their own unique, and at times unhinged, perspective. They are more likely to talk *at* a rock to find its history than wait for it to speak.

## Background

Raised in a monastic tradition on Mythos as an Envoy, Iiko was meant to bridge cultures, though their "unique" personality often made that a challenge. They bonded **Dreamwaker**, an ancient Mistspren whose calm, deep, and soothing voice acts as the anchor to Iiko’s chaotic energy. Since worldhopping to Roshar, Iiko has discovered that fellow survivors **N'tal, Kuma, and Fina** are also from Nuria—a fact they only realized after the transition. Now, Iiko navigates Roshar with a mix of joy and confusion, hoping the Ardentia (specifically the Devotary of Sincerity) can handle their brand of "truth."

## Goals

* **Befriend the Voices:** Discover if the "Possessed" are truly insane, or if they’re just seeing the world as clearly as Iiko does.

* **Join the Ardentia:** Find a group of scholars brave enough to endure their endless questioning and lively theories.

* **Mend the Broken Orders:** Use their powers to prepare the world for the Final Desolation, likely while getting distracted by a particularly interesting spren.

## Connections

* **Dreamwaker:** Their ancient Mistspren partner. He is the velvet-voiced "straight man" to Iiko’s comedy, providing a calm counterpoint to their frantic nature.

* **N'tal, Kuma, and Fina:** Fellow Nurians. They share a homeworld, but Iiko is likely the one making the "bonding experience" the most exhausting.

* **Mallow:** A local from the Purelake. Iiko finds Mallow’s perspective fascinatingly different from their own off-world views.

* **The Truthkeepers:** Their militant employers. Iiko is probably the biggest headache High Witness Yeshan has to deal with.

## Mechanics (Truthwatcher)

Iiko will become a **Truthwatcher** with access to **Progression** and **Illumination**.

* **Truth-Illusions:** Before realizing that their spren bond is the source of the illusions, Iiko and Dreamwaker project "Reflections of Reality." These illusions manifest Iiko’s eccentric perspective—shifting languages, double moons, or ancient lightning—often revealing things others overlook, even if Iiko doesn't realize they're doing it. Dreamwaker builds quiet amusement from this, and hasn't told Iiko yet that they are the source.

* **Growth:** Iiko will eventually be able to use Progression to make flora explode into life, often doing so impulsively because they think a desolated area "looks a bit sad."

* **Weapon:** Quarterstaff (which they likely use more like a walking stick or a pointer for their lectures).
