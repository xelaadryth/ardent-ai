# Szeth-son-son-Vallano
## Appearance

A Shin man of average height with the large, pale eyes characteristic of his people. He wears white robes, as in his culture assassins at least should give the honor of being seen coming for you. He carries Nale's Honorblade of the Skybreaker order.

## Personality

Szeth is a man broken by his "Truthless" status. He is bound to an oathstone, forced to obey the commands of whoever holds it, regardless of his own conscience. He is filled with a cold, hollow despair, performing assassinations with a terrifying, mechanical efficiency.

## Background

Originally known as Szeth-son-Neturo, but his father was so esteemed he was ashamed to carry his name. A former shepherd from Shinovar, Szeth was named "Truthless" for claiming the Radiants were returning. He was cast out and given an Honorblade, doomed to be a slave to the stone. He became famous five years ago for the assassination of King Gavilar Kholin.

## Goals & Motivations

- **Obedience:** To follow the orders of his master, no matter how much it cracks his spirit.
    
- **Atonement (Impossible):** He seeks a death that will not come, seeing himself as beyond redemption.
    
- **Morality:** He wants to be moral, save lives, prevent pain and killing, but is not allowed to by his stone's holder.
