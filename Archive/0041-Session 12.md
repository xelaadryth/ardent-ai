# Following Chip

In the capital city [[FeF1W6cnNv-k8ME9pfXIV|Revolar]] of the Vamah Princedom, the party resolved to track [[AsV00GwhEas4A0eEBDA3k|Chip]] to find out who he and [[YOHcVK8XFMEZiV6HtV1DE|Karani]] are communicating with, what the triangle symbol means, and where their true allegiance lies.

This session focuses on tracking [[AsV00GwhEas4A0eEBDA3k|Chip]] to the [[ZOWhgc_9NJz8OMvKu3X6o|Ghostbloods]], uncovering the tensions between the Ghostbloods and the [[RPZ-tfzIqzRhfboC6cgfN|Bloodmarks]], and beginning an investigation into the [[UosLnowxcsUZ7qi819lHO|Knights of Dusk]] activity around [[FeF1W6cnNv-k8ME9pfXIV|Revolar]] .

## Ghostbloods & Bloodmark Conflict

### The Ghostbloods (The Bruckslats)

Pass through the [[hUQ6_yOrKck133aphc6I-|Stackhouses]]

- **Chip's Meeting**: Chip is reporting to the [[AbDnjy7tS_jZgjDlxKjPC|Undertaker]].
    
- **The Undertaker's Perspective**:
    
    - Interested in **Mythos** (Nuria's home world): A world of humans currently of interest due to gem sources.
        
    - **The Goal**: Move Stormlight off-world to Scadrial. They need high quality gemstones (from Mythos) and the best gemcutters (found on Roshar) to achieve this.
        
    - **Bloodmark Rivalry**: The Bloodmarks' upper echelon is getting into inter-world trade, and they are becoming a cutthroat obstacle.
        
- Example conversation:
    
    - As I mentioned by spanreed earlier, it looks like above the standard Truthkeeper ranks is the [[pWv26vpnMbTxOivgXu5nz|Silent Oath]]. High Enforcer Aylen has a shardblade, and used it to kill the Possessed. Although any Enforcer is technically permitted to kill Possessed, I suspect most will still choose to report to the Silent Oath.
        
        - Do you think they would make good allies?
            
    - From what I've observed, I'd say they still stabilize the region more than they cause risk to us. The [[UosLnowxcsUZ7qi819lHO|Knights of Dusk]] on the other hand will probably be trouble, as they oppose established government. I'll send word if we hear more of their deeper motives though.
        
        - We'll take no open movements for or against them then.
            
    - Also, perhaps [[DAjV-Pu0E_RYt0zQ7NPkm|Possessed]] are controllable. I know of a few Truthkeepers who might be in a tough spot soon; if they're discovered they'll be condemned by their own people, but if they leave...they could be a valuable weapon with no wielder. An opportunity.
        
        - Do you think you can wield such a weapon?
            
    - I wouldn't presume, but I'd rather try to have such a weapon in hand, instead of wielded against us or discarded.
        
        - Very well, see what you can do to...encourage them. Any news on the gems?
            
    - Not yet, I'll see what I can find out. But I can confirm that the reports are true and the traitor is dead. I saw his corpse with my own eyes. What's our stance with the [[RPZ-tfzIqzRhfboC6cgfN|Bloodmarks]] now?
        
        - Just short of open warfare. Mythos set us back, but we must be discreet since [[0YaZuf8_UJeWQ0pZv51rV|Highlord Paladar]] is sharing their spheres.
            
    - I'll be careful. Oh, also I have someone who might might be of interest to you; he's been asking around about the [[AbDnjy7tS_jZgjDlxKjPC|Undertaker]]. Shall I set up a meeting?
        
        - As long as it's within the next two days. Curious...
