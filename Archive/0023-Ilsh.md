# Ilsh
## Appearance

An older Thaylen man in his 60s with iconic, very long white eyebrows and dark blue eyes. He has thinning brown hair and wears the humble robes of an ardent.

## Personality

Exuberant and easily excitable, especially when it comes to "unknown spren." He is a scholar at heart, driven by a manic curiosity about spren and their applications. He is currently pretending to be an ardent because he can read and write in Thaylen, which he uses as a cover for his research.

## Background

A spren researcher from Thaylenah, Ilsh is currently traveling across Roshar—from Alethkar to the Purelake—seeking "horizonspren" and other rare spren types. However after failing to find the horizonspren, he hears news of a fabrial expo in [[JlRniRHuHoKcG8CVD1tbZ|Bodenar]] , and will be present at the:

- **Spren Research:** He is obsessed with finding and studying rare spren for use in advanced fabrials.
    
- **The Bodenar Demonstration:** He is one of the spren researchers on the experimental steamship project.
    
- **Covert Scholasticism:** He uses his "Ardent" status to gain access to libraries and restricted lore.
