## The Conflict

A "storm-blessed" soldier named [[U5_4WXiUyRiMerjS1BXm4|Tien]] is making a name for himself in the brutal border skirmishes of Alethkar and Jah Keved. He is a survivor who seems to defy death at every turn, drawing the attention of both the Heralds and the Radiants. Originally saved by [[2q9FpCMyswSfGC5ko089k|Kaladin's Sacrifice]], which passes on his destiny.

## Key Players

- **Tien:** A former squad leader and potential Radiant who "collects" those in need of protection.
    
- **Helaran:** A mysterious figure sent by the [[ZOWhgc_9NJz8OMvKu3X6o|Ghostbloods]] to assassinate Amaram of the [[7S1PayccxOiXEnZfIcrYN|Sons of Honor]].
    

## Stakes

- **The Discovery of the Radiant:** If Tien is revealed as a Radiant, he becomes a primary target for the Silent Oath.
    
- **The Shardblade:** A Shardblade is won in these battles, but its owner is presumed dead, leading to a tragic shift in the regional power balance.
    

## Progression

- **Act 1: Rumors of the Storm-Blessed:** The party hears stories of a soldier who survived impossible odds in the borderlands.
    
- **Act 2: The Skirmish:** The party witnesses a brutal battle where Tien defends a group of young soldiers (including Dillind).
    
- **Act 3: The Presumed Death:** Tien wins a Shardblade but is betrayed and presumed dead, while the party is left to witness the aftermath of the "execution" of his soldiers.
