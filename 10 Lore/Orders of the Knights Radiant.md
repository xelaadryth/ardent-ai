# Overview
The Knights Radiant were ten distinct Orders of Surgebinders, ancient protectors of Roshar who bonded with spren to manifest supernatural abilities. Each Order was defined by their unique pairing of two fundamental Surges, their specific Nahel spren bond, and a shared set of moral Oaths centered on the First Ideal: "Life before death, strength before weakness, journey before destination."

# The Ten Orders

## 1. Windrunners
- **Herald:** Jezrien
- **Surgebindings:** Adhesion and Gravitation
- **Nahel Spren:** Honorspren
- **Cousin Spren:** Windspren
- **Gemstone:** Sapphire
- **Essence:** Zephyr (Air)
- **Attributes:** Protecting and Leading

## 2. Skybreakers
- **Herald:** [[High Enforcer Aylen]] (Nale)
- **Surgebindings:** Gravitation and Division
- **Nahel Spren:** Highspren
- **Cousin Spren:** Mandras (Luckspren)
- **Gemstone:** Smokestone
- **Essence:** Vapor (Smoke, Fog)
- **Attributes:** Justice and Confidence
- **Note:** The [[Silent Oath]] is an extremist cell of this order operating within the [[Truthkeepers]].

## 3. Dustbringers (Releasers)
- **Herald:** Chanarach
- **Surgebindings:** Division and Abrasion
- **Nahel Spren:** Ashspren (e.g., [[Pyre]])
- **Cousin Spren:** Flamespren
- **Gemstone:** Ruby
- **Essence:** Spark (Fire)
- **Attributes:** Courage and Obedience
- **Members:** [[N'tal]], [[General Spade]]

## 4. Edgedancers
- **Herald:** Vedel
- **Surgebindings:** Abrasion and Progression
- **Nahel Spren:** Cultivationspren (e.g., [[Verdae]])
- **Cousin Spren:** Lifespren
- **Gemstone:** Diamond
- **Essence:** Lucentia (Quartz, Glass)
- **Attributes:** Loving and Healing
- **Members:** [[Fina]]

## 5. Truthwatchers
- **Herald:** Pailiah
- **Surgebindings:** Progression and Illumination
- **Nahel Spren:** Mistspren (e.g., [[Dreamwaker]])
- **Cousin Spren:** Concentrationspren
- **Gemstone:** Emerald
- **Essence:** Pulp (Plant life)
- **Attributes:** Learned and Giving
- **Members:** [[Iiko]], [[Khria]]

## 6. Lightweavers
- **Herald:** Shalash
- **Surgebindings:** Illumination and Transformation
- **Nahel Spren:** Cryptics (Liespren)
- **Cousin Spren:** Creationspren
- **Gemstone:** Garnet
- **Essence:** Blood (Liquids)
- **Attributes:** Creative and Honest
- **Historical Note:** The [[Possessed]] merchant [[Morrow]] was haunted by Cryptics before his execution.

## 7. Elsecallers
- **Herald:** Battar
- **Surgebindings:** Transformation and Transportation
- **Nahel Spren:** Inkspren (e.g., [[Viscose]])
- **Cousin Spren:** Logicspren
- **Gemstone:** Zircon
- **Essence:** Tallow (Oils)
- **Attributes:** Wise and Careful
- **Members:** [[Kuma]], [[Jasnah Kholin]]

## 8. Willshapers
- **Herald:** Kalak
- **Surgebindings:** Transportation and Cohesion
- **Nahel Spren:** Reachers (Lightspren; e.g., [[Cadence]])
- **Cousin Spren:** Joyspren
- **Gemstone:** Amethyst
- **Essence:** Foil (Metals)
- **Attributes:** Resolute and Builder
- **Members:** [[Mallow]]

## 9. Stonewards
- **Herald:** Talenelat
- **Surgebindings:** Cohesion and Tension
- **Nahel Spren:** Peakspren (e.g., [[Kun'ahu]])
- **Cousin Spren:** Bindspren
- **Gemstone:** Topaz
- **Essence:** Talus (Stone)
- **Attributes:** Dependable and Resolver
- **Members:** [[Galeth-son-Thald]]

## 10. Bondsmiths
- **Herald:** [[High Witness Yeshan]] (Ishar)
- **Surgebindings:** Tension and Adhesion
- **Nahel Spren:** Unique (The Stormfather, The Nightwatcher, The Sibling)
- **Cousin Spren:** None
- **Gemstone:** Heliodor
- **Essence:** Sinew (Flesh)
- **Attributes:** Guiding and Instructor
- **Warning:** Current vault intelligence identifies [[High Witness Yeshan]] as the Herald of this Order, though his sanity is severely fractured.
