# Description

A cryptic and epic prophecy delivered to the Mistspren Dreamwaker by another spren named Glys. It outlines the necessity of uniting ten individuals to stave off the Final Desolation.

## Details

The text of the prophecy is as follows:

*When broken orders start to mend,*
*They must unite to stand as ten,*
*As final desolation nears,*
*The voidbringers wake buried fears.*

*Meet bonded six found far from home,*
*Go seek the lost or fall alone.*
*Raise the spear as shield in slaughter,*
*Rejoin fractured herald's daughter,*
*Crown false king to wrongful throne,*
*Bid king killer to atone.*

*When all is lost and end is near,*
*Ten keys shall make the path appear.*
*The missing city must be sought,*
*Else the Almighty died for naught.*

## Implications

This prophecy serves as the primary roadmap for the players. It suggests that the players themselves (the "bonded six") are essential keys. It points toward specific figures they must find: a Windrunner (the spear), a Lightweaver (the daughter), a Bondsmith (the false king), and a Skybreaker (the king killer).

## What People Believe

Currently, this prophecy is unknown to the wider world and even to the Truthkeepers. Iiko's spren Dreamwaker delivered the prophecy to all the players, the "bonded six", which they are keeping a secret to themselves. They have also seem a mural of the Dawncities, which triggered Dreamwaker to remember Glys's prophecy. This was the first time Dreamwaker (or any of the party's spren) talked to the rest of the party.
