# Description
The underlying truth regarding the [[Experimental Steamship]] project: the [[Nurian Gems]] used in its core are intentionally flawed. [[Grand Justicar Malice]] has provided unstable stones to ensure the Thaylen project fails, allowing the [[Bloodmarks]] to buy up the wreckage and the research for a pittance.

# Discovery
- **Technical Check:** A DC 18 Logic or Crafting check by [[Kuma]] or [[Vorn]] reveals the microscopic fractures in the primary gemstone housings.
