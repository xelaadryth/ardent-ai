# Description
Roshar's ecosystem is uniquely adapted to survive the relentless highstorms and the stone-dominated landscape. Most flora can retract into the ground or stone, and most fauna is protected by chitinous shells or specialized carapaces.

## Medicine & Substances
- **Knobweed sap**: A common and effective disinfectant.
- **Lister oil**: An expensive, high-grade disinfectant.
- **Fathom bark**: Used primarily to reduce swelling in joints and limbs.
- **Firemoss**: A potent, highly addictive painkiller often used recreationally.
- **Dazewater**: A laughing gas; its effects can be quickly negated by exposure to fire.
- **Truthberry**: The berries themselves are harmless, but burning the leaves or stalks produces intoxicating smoke.
- **Backbreaker powder**: A lethal poison, though an antidote is available if administered quickly.
- **Ridgebark**: A natural stimulant used to maintain alertness and wakefulness, similar to caffeine.
## Agriculture & Crops
The staple diet of Roshar relies on hardy, stone-grown crops:
- **Grains**: Lavis, Clema, Tallew, and Treb.
- **Tubers**: Longroot is a common staple.
## Trees and Plants
- **Forest Trees**: Vivim trees, Markel trees, and the heavy Stumpweight trees.
- **Lumber**: **Cobwood** is standard for furniture, while **Darkwood** is prized for high-end, luxury pieces.
- **Shrubs & Ground Cover**:
    - **Snarlbrush**: Forms dense, protective thickets.
    - **Scragglebark**: A rough-textured shrub.
    - **Frillbloom**: A fern with two distinctive orange fronds and spiked tips.
    - **Fingermoss**: Characterized by vibrant purple and pink tendrils.
    - **Duskwillow**: A specialized "alerting tree."
    - **Rocklily**: Hardy leaves that grow directly from stone.
    - **Shalebark**: Primarily ornamental; its oil is used to inhibit chull pupation, and its stiff leaves are often used for arrow fletching.
## "Bugs" (Invertebrates)

- **Standard Cremlings**: Haspers, Lanka, and Skrip are found throughout the world.
- **Variant Cremlings**: Redshell Crawlers, Scaleclicks, Fatback Burrowers, Dropfids, Resinbacks, Glowmites, Pebblefracks, Bony Biters, Daskins, Murklicks, and Leafers.
- **Pests**: The **Diggerworm** is a notorious destroyer of crops.
## Animals

- **Apex Predators**: [[KwW8NFI0l-VOu3VGfxH3E|Whitespines]] (vicious, ivory-tusked hunters).
- **Beasts of Burden**: **Chulls** (giant, wagon-pulling crustaceans).
- **Domesticated**: **Axehounds** (six-legged hounds), "Chickens" (any bird), and Minks.
- **Livestock**: Lobberbeasts, Pigs (rare), **Gumfrens** (raised for gemhearts), and various Crabs.
- **Aquatic/Small Life**: Lurgs (amphibians), Snails, and the **Grasper** (a tentacled water-dweller that eats cremlings).
- **Game**: **Telm** (a crustacean-like creature hunted for meat, similar to deer).
- **Minor Fauna**: Burrowcrabs, Cremsnails, Nipshells, and Grazehorns.
