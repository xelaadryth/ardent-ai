# Description
A derogatory or clinical term used by the [[Truthkeepers]] to categorize individuals exhibiting signs of aberrant surges, supernatural abilities, or sudden madness. These individuals are officially suspected of being influenced or controlled by external, malevolent entities.

# Background
The classification of the "Possessed" is a central pillar of Truthkeeper doctrine. The organization views these individuals as an existential threat to the stability of Roshar, fearing that their uncontrolled powers could trigger a repeat of the ancient Desolations. High-ranking officials like [[High Enforcer Aylen]] (Nale) and [[High Witness Yeshan]] (Ishar) utilize the Truthkeeper infrastructure to systematically identify, track, and "neutralize" these targets under the guise of legal and religious purification.
