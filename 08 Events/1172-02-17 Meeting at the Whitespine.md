# Overview
A clandestine meeting held in the deepest, most shadowed booth of the [[Whitespine's Fang]] on the night of 1172-02-17. The meeting serves to vet [[N'tal]] for a direct audience with the [[Undertaker]].

# Atmosphere
- **Sensory Details:** The air is a heavy, visible haze of firemoss smoke that clings to the rough-hewn stone walls like a living thing. Every word spoken in the booth feels like a weight being dropped into a deep well, muffled by the constant, rhythmic "thump-hiss" of a faulty steam-vent in the alleyway outside.
- **Visuals:** The only light comes from a single, dying orange sphere on the table, which catches the ivory of the massive whitespine fang mounted above the bar, making it look like a jagged, predatory grin watching the room. 
- **The Sound of Neutrality:** Despite the tavern's sketchy appearance, it is eerily quiet; the patrons speak in low hums, and the clink of glass is infrequent, reinforcing the unspoken law that business here remains private.

# Participants
- [[N'tal]]: The merchant seeking answers regarding his family.
- [[Chip]]: The smirking Enforcer acting as the bridge between the squad and the syndicate.
- [[Sweeper]]: The nervous but eager contact who is desperate to prove his worth to the [[Ghostbloods]].

# The Discussion
[[Sweeper]] reveals that the [[Undertaker]] has been monitoring the squad's actions in the [[Scarstone Quarter]], specifically their interference in [[1172-02-16 The Stackhouse Slavers]]. He explains that the [[Undertaker]] is not just a merchant but a collector of "functional assets." 

[[Chip]] confirms that the only way to the [[Bruckslats]] base is by invitation, and that the [[Undertaker]] requires a show of "unshakeable commitment" to the Ghostbloods' regional goals before he will reveal what he knows about the [[Bloodmarks]] and the [[Nurian Gems]].

# The Surprise: The Eye in the Rafters
As the meeting concludes, a flash of movement occurs in the dark rafters above the booth. A small, exotic avian (the [[Undertaker]]'s chicken) drops a single, dried leaf onto the center of the table. 

- **The Reveal:** The leaf is from Shinovar; it is a perfectly preserved specimen of a plant that does not retract when touched and lacks the waxy coating of local flora.
- **The Message:** On the underside of the leaf, written in a cramped, elegant hand that only appears when Chip holds it near the heat of a firemoss pipe, is a single word: "INVITED."
- **The Complication:** Just as the group sees the message, a [[Bloodmarks]] Lash enters the tavern. He doesn't attack, but he stands at the bar and slowly scans the room, locking eyes with [[N'tal]] for a fraction of a second longer than necessary before ordering a drink.

# Outcomes
- **The Invitation:** The party now has the "key" to enter the [[Bruckslats]] to meet the [[Undertaker]].
- **Increased Stakes:** The [[Bloodmarks]] are clearly aware of the meeting, ending the squad's period of total anonymity in the [[Driftwards]], but they mistakenly think they're part of the Knights of Dusk and [[Truthkeepers]] like [[Senny]] are brought in.
