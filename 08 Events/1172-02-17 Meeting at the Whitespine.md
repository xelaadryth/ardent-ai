# Overview
When [[N'tal]] walks in, [[Sweeper]] tosses a dart and scores a moderately decent score and is very dramatic. Tells him he got them an audience with the [[Undertaker]]. Reveals his boss is [[Chip]]. In his excitement, he lets slip that [[Chip]]'s real identity is secret and very risky to ask about. Also mentions the [[Undetaker]] likes trophies from distant lands.

# Atmosphere
- **Sensory Details:** The air is a heavy, visible haze of firemoss smoke that clings to the rough-hewn stone walls like a living thing. Every word spoken in the booth feels like it disappears into the ragged curtains around each high-backed booth.
- **The Sound of Neutrality:** Despite the tavern's sketchy appearance, it is eerily quiet; the patrons speak in low hums, and the clink of glass is infrequent, reinforcing the unspoken law that business here remains private.

# Participants
- [[N'tal]]: The merchant seeking answers regarding his family.
- [[Sweeper]]: The overly dramatic but eager contact who is desperate to prove his worth to the [[Ghostbloods]].
