# Description

The Great Betrayal of humanity at Feverstone Keep, where Windrunners and Stonewards killed their spren, and left their Shardblades and Shardplate. Few alive know that it was not a betrayal at all, it was a mutual choice by the spren to lock away Surgebinding after finding out humans were the Voidbringers, in fear of destroying Roshar in the same way as the past.
