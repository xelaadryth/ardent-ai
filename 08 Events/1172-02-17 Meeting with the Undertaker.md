# Overview
Following the invitation at the [[Whitespine's Fang]], the squad is led to the [[Bruckslats]] hideout in the [[Scarstone Quarter]] to meet the [[Undertaker]] (Mraize). The meeting shifts from a standard briefing to a personal crucible for [[N'tal]] and [[Fina]].

# The Meeting
The downstairs basement of the hideout is lush and trophy-filled, with what looks like vials of powder, a bowl of sand, fangs of creatures, a case of pinned butterflies, unfamiliar taxidermized critters, the feathers of a scaled bird, and who knows what else in jars and display cases.

The Undertaker reveals he has the original contracts for the Nurian gem mine discovered by [[N'tal]]'s family. He produces a ledger that tracks the exact moment the [[Bloodmarks]] seized the assets.

# The Revelation
The Undertaker points out that while the Bloodmarks used violence, they acted under a "legal" transfer authorized by the Nurian royal family. He slides the contract across the table, revealing the elegant but hurried signature of an Alethi proxy: [[Fina]]. It becomes clear that Fina’s past negligence in signing documents without reading them provided the legal cover for the Bloodmarks to slaughter N'tal's kin and steal their mine.

# Requests
- Acquire rare and valuable objects; Shin grass is common, he's interested in a Nurian gem sample. Also interested in [[Zabriel]]'s sources ([[Bodenar]]).

# Outcomes
- **Fractured Trust:** The relationship between [[N'tal]] and [[Fina]] is severely strained by the revelation of her role in his family's tragedy.
- **Direct Pursuit:** The Undertaker provides the current location of [[Arbiter Jalis]], the man who carried out the raid, noting he is currently moving a high-value asset near the [[Stackhouses]].
- **Leaving the Nest:** After the party departs the hideout, they find the Bloodmark squad, leading directly to [[1172-02-17 The Foundry Chase]].
