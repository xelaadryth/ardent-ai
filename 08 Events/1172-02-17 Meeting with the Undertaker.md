# Overview
Following the invitation at the Whitespine, the squad is led to the [[Bruckslats]] hideout in the [[Scarstone Quarter]] to meet the [[Undertaker]] (Mraize). The meeting shifts from a standard briefing to a personal crucible for [[N'tal]] and [[Fina]].

# The Meeting
In the lush, trophy-filled sanctum of the hideout, the Undertaker reveals he has the original contracts for the Nurian gem mine discovered by [[N'tal]]'s family. He produces a ledger that tracks the exact moment the [[Bloodmarks]] seized the assets.

# The Revelation
The Undertaker points out that while the Bloodmarks used violence, they acted under a "legal" transfer authorized by the Nurian royal family. He slides the contract across the table, revealing the elegant but hurried signature of an Alethi proxy: [[Fina]]. It becomes clear that Fina’s past negligence in signing documents without reading them provided the legal cover for the Bloodmarks to slaughter N'tal's kin and steal their mine.

# The Twist: The Double Game
As the tension between the party members peaks, the Undertaker reveals that the [[Bloodmarks]] were actually tipping him off about the party's arrival in Revolar. He admits he allowed the meeting to proceed to see if the "broken orders" mentioned in the [[Prophecy of Ten Keys]] would shatter under the weight of their own pasts or unite in their shared trauma.

# Outcomes
- **Fractured Trust:** The relationship between [[N'tal]] and [[Fina]] is severely strained by the revelation of her role in his family's tragedy.
- **Direct Pursuit:** The Undertaker provides the current location of [[Arbiter Jalis]], the man who carried out the raid, noting he is currently moving a high-value asset near the [[Stackhouses]].
- **Leaving the Nest:** As the party departs the hideout, they immediately encounter the Bloodmark squad, leading directly to [[1172-02-17 The Foundry Chase]].
