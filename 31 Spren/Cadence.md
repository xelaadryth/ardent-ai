# Description
A moody and empathetic Lightspren (Reacher) bonded to [[Mallow]]. She is a champion of personal liberty who guides her partner toward the path of a Willshaper.

# Appearance
Cadence manifests as a ball of warm, silky light trailing a comet-like tail. Her luminosity and temperature are dynamic, shifting in intensity based on her current emotional state.

# Personality
She is a complex and often contradictory spirit—deeply kind and patient, yet prone to childlike moodiness and "tsundere" behavior. Above all, she possesses a fierce, unwavering hatred for slavery and any form of forced servitude.

# Background
Hailing from the Cognitive Realm, Cadence was drawn to the specific "cracks" in [[Mallow]]'s soul—the internal conflict between inherited superstition and a burgeoning desire for autonomy. She seeks to mend these fractures by encouraging Mallow to embrace the ideals of freedom.

# Goals
- **Seek Freedom:** Actively pushes her Radiant to transcend past superstitions and the restrictive mandates of the [[Truthkeepers]].
- **Oppose Slavery:** Remains hyper-focused on the plight of the oppressed, specifically targeting the liberation of slaves and parshmen.
