Add in an NPC document for the Truthkeeper Enforcer named Ojog Ojog is 6'4" with light blue eyes. He arrived with the rest of the Nurians on the wagons, but was separated from them after the party was assigned to go north to Hearthstone. He quickly blended in with the Alethi Truthkeepers and made friends.
Back in Nuria, he was studying medicine to be a surgeon, but after saving a wounded soldier, brought disaster upon his town when the soldier turned out to be from an enemy army, who brought critical intel back to the enemies and used the weaknesses discovered in the town's defenses to destroy the town. Carrying that burden, Ojog hesitated to help others with his medical abilities.
Ironically his knowledge of the human anatomy also let him quickly learn to be proficient in dual knives, and for the Truthkeepers he began as a killing machine, taking out enemies of the org almost like an assassin.
Ojog will become a recurring villain to the party; for now he will play the role of the Truthkeeper that catches the party if they ever decide to betray the Truthkeepers. He will become a Skybreaker apprentice to Istrum after Istrum chooses to save him for his "potential" when the Silent Oath wipes out the Knights of Dusk, and will return later when Istrum swears the Third Ideal, to wholly swear allegiance to High Enforcer Aylen, which will pit Ojog directly against the party.
