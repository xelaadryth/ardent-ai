Add an NPC page for Beric Quinn. He has light green eyes, medium length brown hair, and is 5'10" and appears Shin, even though he is also from Nuria and arrived with the party but was separated when they traveled north towards Hearthstone, similar to Ojog.

He has a villager/hunter background, and is adept with a shortbow for hunting wild game. He hates the noble class for their greed, eating and wasting food when others in his village are starving and he fails to keep them fed in a harsh winter. His goal is for no one to go hungry. He is part of the same group as Ojog, but as an Enforcer in Revolar he chooses to defect to the Knights of Dusk immediately, and is one of the spies that the Truthkeepers are searching for. As he is lighteyed, he is viewed as nobility in this world, and it strikes him as supremely wrong that through the sheer accident of being born rich or poor, lighteyed or darkeyed, your destiny is set. He believes in the commonfolk's ability to set their own destiny.
