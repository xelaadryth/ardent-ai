# Summary
A Nurian "maker" with an obsessive drive to repair, seeking the truth of his parents' disappearance in the fabrial industry.

# Appearance
Kuma stands 5'6" and possesses a mix of Shin and Alethi features, allowing him to pass as either depending on his dress. He has dark blue eyes (darkeyed) and thick black hair.

# Personality
Defined by an obsessive drive to create and repair. Whether it's a mundane piece of farm equipment or a legendary Soulcaster, he cannot help but try to improve it. A "maker" in every sense, acting on a creative instinct that his spren, Viscose, finds frustratingly illogical.

# Background
Born to a Nurian farming family, Kuma was always more interested in books and tinkering than the harvest. Raised by his uncle, he was gifted a mysterious device with a large gemstone that began to function only after Kuma infused it with Stormlight. He is traveling to Revolar to find Artifabrians and uncover why his parents left him. Unknown to him, his parents were forced into research for torture-based fabrials.

# Motivations
- Find Parents: Discover where his parents went and why they left him with his uncle.
- Legendary Crafting: To create items of mythic power, surpassing even ancient fabrials.

# Relationships
- [[Viscose]]: His logical and contrarian Inkspren.
- Uncle: The man who raised him and nearly lost his inheritance in a card game.
- The Artifabrians: A group in Revolar he intends to seek out.
