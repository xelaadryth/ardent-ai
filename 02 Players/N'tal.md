# Description
A short Nurian merchant passing as Alethi, armed with knives and beginning the path of a Dustbringer.

# Appearance
Dark hazel eyes and short, wavy dark brown hair. At 4'10", N'tal is remarkably short for an Alethi, compensating with a sharp tongue and an air of intense restraint.

# Personality
Distrustful and cautious. N'tal values patience and self-control above all else, largely to keep the destructive urges of [[Pyre]] in check. He is driven by a focused determination to uncover the truth behind his family's murder.

# Background
A Nurian worldhopper posing as an Alethi merchant, N'tal grew up in a family of foresters and lumber traders. His family's fortunes changed when they discovered a massive vein of high-quality gemstones in their woods. During a brokered deal with the [[Ghostbloods]], the [[Bloodmarks]] violently intervened, seizing the contracts with the complicit support of the Nurian royal family. N'tal recently discovered that this legal theft was enabled by the negligence of [[Fina]], who had authorized the transfer documents without reading them. N'tal survived only because he was away during the subsequent raid, carrying the key to a secret passage.

# Goals
- Find the Undertaker in Revolar and learn the truth about his family's deaths.
- Identify the mysterious man found dead alongside his family.
- Hunt down [[Arbiter Jalis]], the man who participated in his family's slaughter.
- Face the trial of [[1172-02-17 The Foundry Chase]] to manifest his true potential.
