# Summary
A Purelaker seeking atonement for the betrayal that led to her sister's abduction.

# Appearance
Dark green eyes and black hair. She is a Purelaker (Tu Bayla) and wears practical traveling gear.

# Personality
Carries an anxious protectiveness over her animal companion. While she initially claimed to only want to return to the Purelake, she has discovered a surprising wonder in the wider world of Roshar. She is driven by intense guilt.

# Background
Raised by her sister, Khria, until Khria bonded a spren and was taken by the Truthkeepers. Mallow, in a panic, had reported the incident. She joined the Truthkeepers as an "Enforcer" to track them down and save her sister.

# Motivations
- Save Khria: Find her sister and rescue her from the Truthkeepers or her own madness.
- Seek Atonement: Face the betrayal she committed against her sister.
- Protect the Forgotten: She feels a natural kinship with orphans, the poor, and the hungry.

# Relationships
- [[Khria]]: Her sister, a lieutenant in the Knights of Dusk and a Truthwatcher.
- [[Cadence]]: Her moody and empathetic Lightspren (reacher).
- Pom: Her loyal mink companion.
