# Overview
Secretive, highly organized faction dealing in secrets, Investiture, and political manipulation.

# Goals
- **Agenda:** Infiltrate the [[Truthkeepers]], acquire ancient fabrials, and counter the Bloodmarks.
- **Current Posture:** Tracking Nurian gems and seeking revenge for the agent killed on Mythos.

# Structure
- Local Cells: Highly autonomous.
- Initiation: Requires significant favors or valuable items.

# Methods & Influence
Shadow operations, espionage, and black market trade.

# Allies & Rivals
- Chip: Spy embedded in the Truthkeepers.
- Truthkeepers: Target for infiltration.
- Bloodmarks: Criminal rivals.
