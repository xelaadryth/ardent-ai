# Description
The squad arrives at [[House Sathir]] to seek shelter during a Highstorm, navigating local politics, personal training, and the care of vulnerable residents under [[Highlady Marise Sathir]]. The party is currently negotiating an alliance with the Highlady, who governs the eastern region of the Sadeas princedom.

# Events
- **Meeting the Highlady:** The party meets [[Highlady Marise Sathir]] and her stoic guard, [[Mikeyel Franzay]]. [[Highlady Marise Sathir]] suspects the party is linked to political disruptions but offers 1,200 marks in exchange for the recovery of local gemhearts. She provides the squad with a spanreed for remote communication.
- **Estate Life:** The party encounters young Shara Sathir, who is initially shy around the squad. They observe the humane treatment of parshmen and learn of the estate's effort to protect orphans from conscription.
- **Highstorm Downtime:** During the three-day storm, the party undergoes personal growth and training:
  - **[[N'tal]]:** Receives discipline training from [[Mikeyel Franzay]], learning to channel his passions to avoid self-destruction.
  - **[[Kuma]]:** Improves fabrication skills under guidance from estate scholars, crafting gear for the party.
  - **[[Mallow]]:** Spends time with the orphans, focusing on her desire to protect the forgotten.
  - **[[Iiko]]:** Iiko (they/them) sought a quiet space to meditate and believed they found a serene temple. In reality, they were in a garden shed. Their spren, [[Dreamwaker]], used the surge of Illumination and knowledge of the future (sourced from the spren [[Glys]]) to project a vision of the temple from [[006 Dillind and the Temple]]. [[Mallow]] witnessed Iiko walk into the shed but chose not to intervene.
  - **[[Galeth-son-Thald]]:** Receives combat instruction from [[Mikeyel Franzay]], learning polearm and armor stances.
  - **[[Fina]]:** Conducts historical research into the post-Hierocracy period.
