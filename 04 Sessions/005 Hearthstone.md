# Description
The squad departs the [[House Sathir|Sathir Estate]] after a final highstorm and arrives in the remote town of [[Hearthstone]], where they encounter the local medical crisis, political tension under the [[Toralin Roshone|Roshones]], and a potential trail of missing shipments.

# Scenes
- **Departure from Sathir:** As the final highstorm passes, the party notes their dun spheres and prepares to leave. Before departing, they visit the Whitespine den, noting the tusks have been removed—a potential setup for framing. They secure 1200 marks for their gemhearts and receive word of upcoming recruitment drives for [[Amaram]].
- **Arrival in Hearthstone:** A remote agricultural hub in the year 1172.1.5.4. The squad socializes at "The Hearth" tavern. [[Ilsh Thaylen]] prepares to depart with a merchant caravan. 
- **The Surgeon's Plight:** The party meets [[Lirin]], a surgeon mourning his lost sons, who requires Lister's oil. [[Fina]] finds an opportunity to assist him in his clinic.
- **Political Tension:** The squad meets [[Toralin Roshone]] and [[Laral Roshone]]. [[Laral Roshone]] is attempting to manage the town's politics and recognizes the party, harboring lingering thoughts of the past.
- **Camp Connections:** The party reunites with [[Renna Palir]], [[Chip]], and [[Karani]]. The [[Truthkeepers]] are investigating missing shipments; [[Chip]]'s intelligence has led them to believe the trail ends here.

# Hooks
- **The Merchant Morrow:** A mysterious, terrified man hiding on the town borders. The party must decide whether to respect legal boundaries or assist him.
- **Runaway Slaves:** Local thugs are capturing runaways. The party faces a moral dilemma: return them to [[Citylord Nilar Yenev]]'s strict governance or assist them via legal loopholes and aid from [[House Sathir]].
- **Strange Lightning:** [[Iiko]] observes unnatural lightning during a rapid highstorm, hinting at deeper atmospheric anomalies.
