# Description
The squad departs the [[House Sathir|Sathir Estate]] after a final highstorm and arrives in the remote town of [[Hearthstone]], where they encounter the local medical crisis, political tension under the [[Toralin Roshone|Roshones]], and a potential trail of missing shipments.

# Events
- **Departure from [[House Sathir]]:** As the final highstorm passes, the party notes their dun spheres and prepares to leave. Before departing, they visit the Whitespine den, noting the tusks have been removed—a potential setup for framing. They secure 1200 marks for their gemhearts and receive word of upcoming recruitment drives for [[Amaram]]. [[Iiko]] experienced hallucinations during the downtime, perceiving a shed as a shrine with an eye.
- **Stringken Revisited:** The party returned to the cave near [[Stringken]] to collect the remaining Whitespine remains. They discovered signs of interference and that the tusks had been stolen, though they recovered claws, carapace, and bone.
- **Arrival in Hearthstone:** A remote agricultural hub in the year 1172.1.5.4. The squad socializes at "The Hearth" tavern. [[Ilsh]] prepares to depart with a merchant caravan. A patron reports being stopped by soldiers in unknown gray and brown uniforms.
- **The Surgeon's Plight:** The party meets [[Lirin]] and his wife [[Hesina]] to treat [[Galeth-son-Thald]]'s injuries. [[Fina]] finds an opportunity to assist in the clinic.
- **Political Tension:** The squad meets [[Toralin Roshone]] and [[Laral Roshone]]. The party reunites with [[Renna Palir]], [[Chip]], [[Tusa]], and [[Pafo]].
- **The Merchant Morrow:** They encounter a disoriented man named Morrow living on the east side of town in rags. He suffers from hallucinations of spiral-headed men chasing him. [[Iiko]] notes mentions of "liespren" in relation to his condition. The party brings him into protective custody.
