# Description
The squad returns to the Truthkeeper Camp to receive promotions from Adjudicator Peton before meeting the enigmatic High Enforcer Aylen. They then prepare for a journey toward Revolar, passing through various settlements while navigating deeper conspiracies.

# Scenes
- **Promotions:** [[Adjudicator Peton]] formally elevates the squad to the rank of Enforcer, granting them 300 clearmarks and revealing their service to the [[Silent Oath]].
- **The High Enforcer:** [[High Enforcer Aylen]] arrives, confirming [[High Witness Yeshan]]'s disappearance. He demonstrates the cold "cleansing" process by executing [[Morrow the Merchant]], labeling him a carrier of the "contagion" of the [[Possessed]].
- **New Orders:** Peton offers the squad the choice to continue their service. They are tasked with traveling to [[Revolar]] to support Vamah princedom troops, investigating the [[Knights of Dusk]], and potentially performing favors for the [[Ghostbloods]] to gain entry credentials.
- **Salimset Encounter:** [[N'tal]] is contacted by the Sweeper, who offers an audience with the [[Undertaker]] in [[Revolar]] in exchange for information on how the Truthkeepers process the Possessed.
- **The Journey South:** The squad travels through [[Branchken]], [[Leafken]], [[Listern]], [[Climit]], [[Yola]], and [[Shess]], witnessing the widespread poverty and economic strain caused by high taxes and labor recruitment.

# Hooks
- **Revolar Infiltration:** Reach the city, locate the Knights of Dusk base, and obtain credentials.
- **The Undertaker's Audience:** Meet the Sweeper at the Whitespine's Fang to arrange the meeting with the Undertaker.
- **Branchken Logistics:** Investigate the costly trade environment at the Splitdocks and Dungmarket.

# Connections
- [[High Enforcer Aylen]]: A terrifying Herald whose presence signals a shift in the Truthkeepers' mission.
- [[Undertaker]]: The primary target for N'tal's quest for truth regarding his family.
- [[Revolar]]: The next major theater of operations.
