# Description
The squad is formally promoted to Enforcer rank at the Truthkeeper Camp by [[Adjudicator Peton]] and introduced to the [[Silent Oath]] via [[High Enforcer Aylen]]. Following the execution of [[Morrow the Merchant]], the party prepares for their transfer to [[Revolar]].

# Scenes
- **The Promotion:** [[Adjudicator Peton]] acknowledges the squad's competence, promoting them to rank 10 Enforcers and awarding them 30 ruby marks. He reveals the Truthkeepers' allegiance to the [[Silent Oath]].
- **The Herald's Arrival:** [[High Enforcer Aylen]] arrives to oversee the transition. He confirms that [[High Witness Yeshan]] is missing and that leadership will be restructured. 
- **The Purge:** Aylen executes [[Morrow the Merchant]] for alleged contact with Cryptics, demonstrating the brutal efficiency of the Silent Oath in suppressing the "Possessed."
- **Path Forward:** The party is given the choice to continue service or take a break. They prepare to head to [[Revolar]], where they will investigate the [[Knights of Dusk]] and handle political debt to the [[Ghostbloods]].

# Hooks
- **Revolar Infiltration:** Establish credentials to enter the city; bribe the gate guard Jokrun.
- **Ghostblood Favor:** Track Bloodmark agents or delay trade agreements to earn favor with the Ghostbloods.
- **Knights of Dusk:** Locate the rebel base within Revolar to dismantle their influence.

# Connections
- [[High Enforcer Aylen]]: A terrifying authority figure who embodies the Silent Oath's mission.
- [[Adjudicator Peton]]: Now acting as a mentor figure for the party's future.
- [[Revolar]]: The primary hub for the squad's upcoming operations.
