# Description
The squad is formally promoted to Enforcers by [[Adjudicator Peton]] before witnessing a chilling demonstration of the [[Silent Oath]]'s methods. They prepare to transfer to [[Revolar]], navigating political tensions and new contacts.

# Scenes
- **The Promotion:** [[Adjudicator Peton]] acknowledges the squad's competence, promoting them to Rank 10 Enforcers and revealing their service to the [[Silent Oath]]. 
- **The High Enforcer:** [[High Enforcer Aylen]] arrives, introducing himself and the [[Silent Oath]]. During the assembly, [[Morrow]] is brought forward and summarily executed by Aylen using a Shardblade to 'purge the contagion,' revealing the brutal truth behind the Truthkeepers' mission.
- **Political Briefing:** Aylen discusses the instability in Azir and Tukar. The squad is given the choice to continue their service or rotate, with their ultimate objective shifting to [[Revolar]] to deal with the [[Knights of Dusk]] rebellion.
- **Travel & Logistics:** The party maps a route through [[Salimset]], [[Branchken]], [[Leafken]], [[Listern]], [[Climit]], [[Yola]], and [[Shess]], noting the economic hardships of the region and potential smuggling routes via [[Ghostbloods]] contacts.

# Hooks
- **Revolar Infiltration:** The squad must infiltrate [[Revolar]], with options ranging from legal military reporting to bribery via [[Jokrun]] at the North Gate.
- **Sweeper's Offer:** [[N'tal]] is invited to meet the [[Undertaker]] in [[Revolar]] to learn the truth about the [[Possessed]].
- **Ghostblood Favor:** Infiltrating the Knights of Dusk base may require trading intelligence with the [[Ghostbloods]] regarding Bloodmark trade agreements.

# Connections
- [[High Enforcer Aylen]]: A Herald acting as the primary antagonist/authority figure.
- [[Morrow]]: A merchant eliminated for 'possession' by Cryptics.
- [[Istrim]]: A witness/official observing global unrest.
- [[Sweeper]]: An informant linking the squad to the [[Ghostbloods]].
