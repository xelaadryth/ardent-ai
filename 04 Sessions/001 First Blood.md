# Description
Following a tragic death in their ranks, the squad must navigate a moral and disciplinary crisis before heading into the field. Their loyalty is tested during their first official mission to investigate rumors in [[Hearthstone]], culminating in a desperate survival scenario against local fauna.

# Events
- **The Verdict:** In the wake of Sila's fatal actions, [[Adjudicator Peton]] forces the squad to decide the traitor's fate. Investigation of Sila's body reveals a Ghostblood symbol, directly linking him to the murder of [[N'tal]]'s family.
- **The Separation:** While the primary squad is assigned to the investigation in [[Hearthstone]], [[Ojog]] is detached from the group and remains at the [[Truthkeeper Camp]] to serve as a specialized enforcer under the direct command of [[Adjudicator Peton]].
- **The Mercenary Contract:** The squad learns that [[Luggan]] was paid 3000 marks by a mysterious benefactor to assassinate [[Doug (Guddy)]]. 
- **The Evidence:** [[Doug (Guddy)]] possessed a cryptic note referencing the "Brightest Inn" in [[Revolar]], mentions of the second moon, the "taker’s plans," and betrayal, all marked with a specific Ghostblood symbol.
- **The Mission Brief:** To secure their requested transfer to [[Revolar]], the group is assigned to [[Hearthstone]] to debunk or confirm a reported "miracle" that may indicate the presence of the "Possessed."
- **Camp Observation:** The squad observed a terrifying, ominous figure walking with [[High Witness Yeshan]] within the [[Truthkeeper Camp]].
- **The Recovery:** Two hours from the camp, the party encountered [[Heala]], the brother of the quartermaster, on a firemoss bender. They took him into their custody; he appeared entirely ordinary, contrary to claims of possession.
- **Departure and Ambush:** During the two-week caravan journey to [[Hearthstone]], the squad successfully repelled a swarm of Shellmites.
