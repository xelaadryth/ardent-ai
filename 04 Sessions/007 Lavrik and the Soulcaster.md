# Description
The squad pushes deeper into the ancient temple, confronting [[Lavrik]] and an ardent named [[Ardent Vedelis]] as they attempt to secure an ancient Soulcaster and relay intelligence to the [[Knights of Dusk]].

# Scenes
- **The Temple Interior:** The party enters the collapsed cathedral, finding a damp environment reclaimed by cave creatures. They navigate a treacherous path across a fallen statue under a waterfall. [[Iiko]] spots movement on the second floor.
- **The Confrontation:** Inside the main room, [[Lavrik]] is discovered behind stone furniture, providing a captured object to the ardent [[Ardent Vedelis]], who attempts to transmit reports to [[General Spade]] via spanreed.
- **Boss Battle:** 
  - **Enemies:** Four spearmen and two archers on the balcony.
  - **Tactics:** [[Lavrik]] wields a longspear, succumbing to the Thrill at half-health, granting him bloodlust and combat bonuses. [[Ardent Vedelis]] struggles with the Soulcaster, attempting to transmute matter into wood before fleeing.
  - **The Squad:** [[N'tal]] struggles with discipline during the fray, accidentally harming a noncombatant. [[Galeth-son-Thald]] utilizes stoneshaping, [[Kuma]] employs transmutation, and [[Iiko]] uses illusions to disrupt enemy formations.
- **The Aftermath:** The party secures a tattered book written in Dawnchant found in a drawer. Other documents in the archives crumble to dust upon contact.

# Hooks
- **The Soulcaster's Function:** Further investigation is needed to understand the object [[Ardent Vedelis]] was attempting to activate.
- **Dawnchant Knowledge:** The recovered book requires expert translation to unlock the temple's secrets.
- **The Thrill:** [[Lavrik]]’s surrender to the Thrill indicates a darkening corruption in the region.
