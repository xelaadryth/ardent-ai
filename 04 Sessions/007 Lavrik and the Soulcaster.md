# Description
The squad pushes deeper into the ancient temple, confronting [[Lavrik]] and an ardent named [[Ardent Vedelis]] as they attempt to secure an ancient Soulcaster and relay intelligence to the [[Knights of Dusk]].

# Events
- **The Temple Interior:** The party enters the collapsed cathedral, finding a damp environment reclaimed by cave creatures. They navigate a treacherous path across a fallen statue under a waterfall. [[Iiko]] spots movement on the second floor.
- **The Cave River:** Investigating further, the party finds a cave river leading from the temple interior into a massive, hidden subterranean cavern filled with enemy forces.
- **The Confrontation:** The party attempts to infiltrate the cavern, but their prisoner [[Lod]] blows their cover. A chaotic combat ensues. The squad faces an ardent utilizing a Soulcaster to transmute matter into smoke.
- **The Aftermath:** The squad defeats the enemy forces and secures the Soulcaster and a spanreed. Before dying, [[Lavrik]] reveals his group discovered the artifact in the temple and had an ardent capable of manipulating it. [[Fina]] attempts to use the captured spanreed to impersonate the ardent to the recipient, though the ruse may be suspect.
- **The Ancient Archives:** The party secures a tattered book written in Dawnchant found in a drawer. Other documents in the archives crumble to dust upon contact.
