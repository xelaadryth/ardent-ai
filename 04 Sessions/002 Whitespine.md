# Description
Following an investigation into a local "plague" in the town of [[Hobbleken]], the squad travels to [[Stringken]], where they encounter spren researchers and take on a contract to hunt a dangerous Whitespine.

# Scenes
- **Hobbleken Interlude:** [[Fina]] delivers a message to [[Renna Palir]] and departs with the non-player entourage. The squad investigates rumors of a supernatural illness in [[Hobbleken]], ultimately identifying it as a common cold from Nurian travelers. They assist with local disputes before a highstorm forces them to seek shelter.
- **The Road to Stringken:** The party arrives in [[Stringken]] amidst reports of increased forced recruitment by [[Highlord Sadeas]].
- **Spren Research:** At the "Creaky Chull" tavern, the group meets [[Ilsh Thaylen]], a researcher documenting spren, and discusses local trade and lore.
- **The Whitespine Hunt:** Investigating reports of missing livestock, the party tracks a beast to a cave. They successfully smoke out and defeat a Whitespine, discovering skeletons that link the incident to [[Highlord Paladar]]’s allies.

# Hooks
- **Missing Merchants:** The merchant Morrow has been missing for months; rumors place him traveling south toward Salimset.
- **Political Tension:** The conflict between land owners and squatters in [[Hobbleken]] and the aggressive recruiting by [[Highlord Sadeas]] suggest a region on the brink of deeper unrest.
- **Highwaymen Conspiracy:** The skeletons found in the Whitespine den reveal that [[Highlord Paladar]]'s Vamah allies are operating as highwaymen in the area.
