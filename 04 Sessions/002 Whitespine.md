# Description
The squad navigates a diplomatic and investigative mission through the towns of [[Hobbleken]] and [[Stringken]], involving local disease investigation, agricultural disputes, and the hunting of a dangerous Whitespine.

# Scenes
- **Hobbleken Arrival:** [[Fina]] delivers a message to [[Renna Palir]]. The squad investigates a mysterious "plague" in the town, diagnosing it as a common cold from outsiders. They navigate local social tensions between farmers and landowners while waiting out a highstorm.
- **The Trade Route:** The squad investigates rumors of missing merchants like Morrow and takes on odd jobs, including pest control and escorting lavis grain deliveries toward [[Stringken]].
- **Stringken Tavern:** At the "Creaky Chull," the squad meets Ilsh, a Thaylen spren researcher and Stormwarden, who aids them in their exploration of the local fauna.
- **The Whitespine Hunt:** Investigating signs of a large beast near the ranchlands—broken fences and large footprints—the squad tracks a Whitespine to a cave den. With the help of archers and Ilsh, they smoke out the beast and recover two gumfrem gemhearts.
- **Grim Discoveries:** Inside the Whitespine den, the party discovers skeletal remains, later identified as highwaymen aligned with [[Highlord Paladar]]’s Vamah interests.

# Hooks
- **Missing Merchants:** Morrow and other merchants remain missing; the trail leads south toward Salimset.
- **Sadeas Recruitment:** The ongoing forced recruitment of youths for border skirmishes is causing instability in the region.
- **Vamah Connections:** The discovery of Paladar’s highwaymen in the Whitespine den suggests broader political hostility toward the party.

# Connections
- [[Fina]]: Key point of contact for Renna Palir and the investigation.
- [[Ilsh]]: Researcher and ally in the Whitespine hunt.
- [[Hobbleken]]: Site of the initial investigation and storm shelter.
- [[Stringken]]: Destination and site of the beast hunt and tavern investigation.
