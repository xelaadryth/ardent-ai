# Description
The squad decides to formally break their ties with the [[Truthkeepers]] to assist the [[Knights of Dusk]] in a daring rescue operation in the [[Stackhouses]]. The defection results in a tense confrontation where the party must choose their future.

# Events
- **The Rescue Mission:** The squad receives word that a young teenage boy is being held by the [[Truthkeepers]] as a suspected Possessed. They coordinate with [[Beric Quinn]] to liberate him.
- **The Confrontation:** At the rendezvous point, [[Ojog]] intercepts the party. He believes they are executing a Truthkeeper mission until the party reveals their intent to defect. The betrayal cuts deep for Ojog, who trusted them as fellow survivors.
- **Renna's Choice:** As tensions peak and Ojog prepares to strike, [[Renna Palir]] steps between the party and Ojog. With everyone holding their breath, she draws her blade—not against the party, but to defend them, revealing her true loyalty to the [[Knights of Dusk]].
- **Beric's Sacrifice:** During the ensuing struggle against [[Truthkeepers]] enforcers, [[Beric Quinn]] manifests surges consistent with a budding Windrunner. He takes a lethal strike intended for the squad, allowing the group and the rescued boy to escape.
- **The Aftermath:** The squad fully integrates into the [[Knights of Dusk]] hideout in the [[Darkstone Forest]], leaving the Truthkeeper life behind.

# Connections
- [[Ojog]]: A former ally now betrayed.
- [[Beric Quinn]]: A fallen hero whose sacrifice solidifies their new path.
- [[Renna Palir]]: An ally whose allegiance is finally confirmed.
