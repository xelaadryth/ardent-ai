# Description
A high-ranking official within the Truthkeepers who balances a rigid sense of duty with the messy reality of leading "outsider" units. He is currently furious at the party for causing trouble.

# Appearance
Tall and sharp-faced with bright blue eyes and a neatly cropped hair and beard. He is typically seen equipped for duty, wearing a sidesword and carrying a shield. (Enforcer 3).

# Personality
A rigid traditionalist who believes lighteyes exist to guide the darkeyed. He struggles to adapt to the diverse and often chaotic ranks of the Truthkeepers, opting to do things strictly by the book, which leads him to manage the "outsider" squads that no one else wants. He respects democratic processes when strictly dictated by rules.

# Background
Originally an Enforcer, Peton’s dedication to the law saw him promoted to the rank of Adjudicator. He assigns himself to difficult or unconventional units to ensure they remain aligned with the law.

# Goals
- **Maintaining Order:** Ensure society functions by established rules and traditional structures.
- **Leadership:** Guide his "flock" toward the Truthkeeper cause through discipline and example.
