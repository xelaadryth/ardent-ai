# Description
A mysterious figure and highly skilled assassin operating on behalf of the [[Ghostbloods]]. He has been dispatched to eliminate [[Amaram]].

# Personality
Cold, professional, and single-minded. He operates with a level of precision that suggests high-level training or supernatural enhancement.

# Background
Helaran's past is shrouded in secrecy, but he has risen quickly within the [[Ghostbloods]] hierarchy as a specialized wetwork agent. His current contract involves the destabilization of the [[Sons of Honor]] through the removal of their primary local leader.

# Goals
- **Assassination:** Successfully eliminate [[Amaram]] as ordered by the [[Ghostbloods]].
- **Anonymity:** Complete his mission without revealing his identity to the [[Truthkeepers]] or [[Knights of Dusk]].

# Connections
- [[Ghostbloods]]: His patrons and handlers.
- [[Amaram]]: His primary target.
- [[03 Stormblessed Arc]]: Key player in the border conflict.
