# Description
A 14-year-old boy attempting to escape his parents, who are members of a cult obsessed with reviving the Lost Radiants.

# Personality
He maintains a forced, likable demeanor to avoid pain and danger. He is deeply vulnerable and emotionally fragile, secretly weeping when he believes he is unobserved. He longs for a family that loves him for his own sake.
