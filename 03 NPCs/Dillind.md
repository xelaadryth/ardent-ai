# Description
A 14-year-old boy who successfully escaped his parents, Milam and Fari, who were members of the Envisagers cult.

# Personality
He maintains a forced, likable demeanor to avoid pain and danger. He is deeply vulnerable and emotionally fragile, secretly weeping when he believes he is unobserved. He longs for a family that loves him for his own sake. After his liberation, he has begun training to seek his own path, eventually falling in with [[Tien]].
