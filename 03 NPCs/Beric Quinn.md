# Description
A Nurian hunter and former [[Truthkeepers|Truthkeeper]] Enforcer who defected to the [[Knights of Dusk]] to fight against the systemic greed and inequality of the Alethi caste system. He is a budding Windrunner that tries to protect those who cannot protect themselves.

# Appearance
Beric stands 5'10" with light green eyes and medium-length brown hair. Despite his Nurian origin, he appears Shin to the people of Roshar, which he occasionally uses to deflect suspicion regarding his combat skills. Occasionally windspren can be seen following his arrows.

# Personality
Beric is driven by a deep-seated hatred for the noble class, whom he views as gluttonous and wasteful while the commonfolk starve. He is a staunch egalitarian who believes that destiny is not dictated by the color of one's eyes but by individual action and community support. He possesses a hunter's patience and a villager's pragmatism, dedicated to protecting the weak and defenseless.

# Background
Originally a hunter and provider for his village in Nuria, Beric arrived on Roshar alongside the other Nurians ([[N'tal]], [[Kuma]], and [[Ojog]]). Like [[Ojog]], he was separated from the main squad upon arrival at the [[Truthkeeper Camp]]. Initially recruited as an Enforcer due to his archery skills, he was horrified by the Truthkeepers' methods and the blatant disregard for the starving poor in [[Revolar]]. He defected almost immediately upon reaching the city, joining the [[Knights of Dusk]] to act as an internal spy.

# Goals
- Eradicate hunger within the [[Stackhouses]] and other impoverished districts.
- Dismantle the lighteyed/darkeyed social hierarchy.
- Provide intelligence to [[General Spade]] regarding [[Truthkeepers]] movements.

# Hooks
- **The Internal Threat:** Beric is one of the primary "spies" currently being hunted by [[Adjudicator Peton]]'s forces in [[Revolar]].
- **The Equality of the Bow:** Beric believes the bow is the ultimate equalizer, as it does not care about the rank of the person it strikes.

# Connections
- [[Ojog]]: A fellow Nurian survivor who took the opposite path of rigid duty within the [[Truthkeepers]].
- [[Knights of Dusk]]: The organization he serves as a mole.
