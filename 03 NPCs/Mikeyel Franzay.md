# Description
Head of guard for House Sathir. A towering 6'8" warrior of immense physical presence, fiercely loyal to Marise, though he hides his affection for her.

# Personality
Literal, serious, and devoid of humor. He struggles with the survivor's guilt of losing his brother during a bandit skirmish involving a legendary halberd.

# Goals
- Protect House Sathir at all costs.
- Guide the party in tempering their emotional passions.

# Connections
- [[Highlady Marise Sathir]]: His liege and the secret object of his devotion.
