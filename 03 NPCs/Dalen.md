# Description
A gruff, fiercely loyal darkeyed steward of [[House Sathir]] who carries the weight of a soldier's past and a protector's heart. He has traveled to [[Revolar]] to act as the eyes and ears of [[Highlady Marise Sathir]] and ensure her interests are protected during the rising conflict.

# Appearance
Early 50s with dark brown eyes and a weathered face that belies his gentle nature. He wears well-maintained steward's robes. A significant leg injury from his soldiering days gives him a permanent limp, a constant reminder of the battles that prevented him from joining [[Highlord Kethren Sathir]] at the Shattered Plains.

# Personality
Dalen presents a "rough and tumble" exterior, often speaking with the bluntness of a veteran. However, he is a "sweetie" inside, known as "Uncle Dalen" to the children of the estate and trusted by the villagers. He is a devout Vorin who frequently invokes the Heralds. He is driven by an unshakeable sense of duty and a desire to help those in need without expectation of reward.

# Background
A former soldier and close friend of Aljun Sathir (Kethren’s father), whom he looked up to before Aljun died in battle. Dalen swore to protect Kethren in his stead, but his mobility-limiting injuries forced him into a domestic role. He became the backbone of [[House Sathir]]'s administration. He has now come to [[Revolar]] as a representative of the Highlady, offering a rare source of genuine kindness in the city.

# Goals
- Represent [[Highlady Marise Sathir]]'s interests in [[Revolar]].
- Assist the party as a reliable ally without requiring explicit payment.
- Keep a watchful eye on the [[Knights of Dusk]] and the city leadership.

# Hooks
- **Old Friends:** Dalen has old friends in the [[Revolar]] guard on both the city leaders' side and within the [[Knights of Dusk]] insurgency.
- **Soldier's Heart:** Despite his physical limitations, Dalen still feels the urge to fight and may put himself in harm's way to protect his charges.

# Connections
- [[Highlady Marise Sathir]]: His liege and the person he serves directly.
- [[Highlord Kethren Sathir]]: The noble he swore to protect.
