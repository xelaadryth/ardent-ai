# Description
A former merchant from [[Revolar]] who was haunted by supernatural phenomena. He was identified by the [[Truthkeepers]] as [[Possessed]] and was subsequently executed by [[High Enforcer Aylen]].

# Appearance
Morrow is a man who looks older than his years, with a tangled beard and eyes that dart frantically as if watching something invisible in the air. He wears the travel-stained clothes of a merchant, though they are now little more than rags.

# Personality
Confused and terrified, he often speaks in half-finished sentences or mutters to the "shadows" following him. He is inherently kind, often trying to share his meager scraps of food with others, even while in a state of distress. He is driven by a desire to do good to atone for a shadowy past.

# Background
Once a merchant who found himself embroiled in dark dealings, Morrow turned his life around to become an honest man. However, his "snapping" occurred recently, and he began to see strange, geometric figures ([[Cryptics]]) that seem to whisper truths about his past. He was captured in Salimset by the [[Truthkeepers]].

# Goals
- **Atonement:** To perform enough good in the world to outweigh the secrets he carries—secrets the [[Cryptics]] find fascinating.

# Hooks
- **Hidden Past:** The nature of the "dark dealings" he was involved in remains a mystery that haunted him until his end.

# Connections
- [[High Enforcer Aylen]]: Captured Morrow in Salimset and executed him for "possession."
- **Rhilon**: A local shopkeep in [[Revolar]] who remembers him as a kind merchant who once exported grain.
