# Description
A quiet, observant darkeyed laundry-worker in the [[Slipmarket]] who serves as a vital informant for the [[Knights of Dusk]].

# Appearance
Lila has the rough, red hands of someone who spends all day over steaming tubs of soap and water. She has short, dark hair usually kept back with a simple kerchief and a nervous habit of smoothing her apron when she speaks.

# Personality
Initially timid and soft-spoken, Lila possesses a core of steely resolve. She is deeply loyal to the [[Knights of Dusk]] and views her small acts of espionage as her way of fighting back against the regime that nearly destroyed her.

# Background
Lila was a modest seamstress in the [[Fieldwards]] until a corrupt Vamah tax collector falsely accused her of under-reporting income to seize her shop. She was being transported to the [[Stackhouse Slavers]] when a [[Knights of Dusk]] strike team, led by a disguised [[Khria]], intercepted the caravan. They freed the captives and provided Lila with a new identity and a job in the [[Slipmarket]] laundry service used by the city guard.

# Goals
- Provide accurate intelligence on military movements to [[General Spade]] by listening to guards in the [[Slipmarket]].
- Keep her cousin, a scribe in [[The Vamah Archivum]], safe from suspicion.
- Protect her community in the [[Stackhouses]] from [[Bloodmarks]] debt collectors.

# Hooks
- **Laundry Secrets:** Lila has found a blood-stained note in a [[Truthkeepers]] uniform that mentions a planned raid on a "red laundry" house.
- **The Archivum Link:** She can introduce the party to her cousin, who has access to the restricted maps within [[The Vamah Archivum]].
- **Hidden Shelter:** If the party is pursued, she knows a hidden crawlspace behind the industrial wash-vats in the [[Slipmarket]].

# Secrets
- Lila is currently hiding a wounded rebel messenger in her small room in the [[Stackhouses]].
- She has been stealing small, dun spheres from the pockets of drunk guards to fund the rebellion's medical supplies.

# Connections
- [[Khria]]: The leader she reports to and views as a guardian.
