# Description
An Ardent of Vamah who served as a trusted advisor to [[Lavrik]]. He was a man of quiet conviction who believed that preserving the historical and religious truth of Roshar was a sacred duty.

# Personality
Vedelis was gentle, studious, and deeply committed to the ideals of the Devotary of Sincerity. Unlike many of his peers, he possessed a keen moral compass, which eventually led him to oppose the corruption within the Vamah princedom.

# Background
Vedelis grew up studying alongside [[Ardent Kaelis]] in the Devotary of Sincerity. When he noticed that political agents loyal to [[Highlord Paladar]] were systematically removing and destroying books from local libraries, he became convinced that a deliberate campaign to erase history was underway. This discovery forced him to align with [[General Spade]] and the [[Knights of Dusk]], viewing the rebellion as a desperate measure to preserve the truth from those who sought to bury it.

# Connections
- [[Lavrik]]: His close associate; they worked together to secure ancient artifacts and relay intelligence to the [[Knights of Dusk]].
- [[Ardent Kaelis]]: A lifelong friend and fellow scholar of the Devotary of Sincerity. They shared a dedication to objective truth.
- [[General Spade]]: The leader whose cause Vedelis supported to counteract the corruption of House Paladar.

# Hooks
- Investigation into the specific texts Vedelis was trying to protect remains a priority for those who value history.
- His work with the Soulcaster in his final moments suggests he was attempting to secure a key piece of lost knowledge before the temple fell.
