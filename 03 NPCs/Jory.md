# Description
A terrified teenage boy secretly working for the [[Knights of Dusk]] who has recently begun hearing the whispers of a spren.

# Appearance
Small and thin for his age, with messy hair and eyes that constantly dart toward unseen shadows.

# Personality
Vulnerable and overwhelmed by his burgeoning abilities. He is deeply traumatized by his capture by the [[Bloodmarks]].

# Background
Recruited by the [[Knights of Dusk]] after his "possession" was discovered in the [[Stackhouses]]. He was captured by [[Arbiter Jalis]] during a transfer operation and held for interrogation regarding the rebel hideouts.

# Goals
- Survive his captivity.
- Understand the voices in his head.

# Connections
- [[Knights of Dusk]]: The organization that attempted to protect him.
- [[Arbiter Jalis]]: His captor.
