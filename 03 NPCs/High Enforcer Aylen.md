# Appearance
Aylen is a man of intense, terrifying stillness. He has dark eyes, dark skin and a face that seems carved from obsidian, marked by a precise, crescent moon scar on his cheek. He wears the formal, stiff blacks of the [[Silent Oath]], and his presence feels like the weight of a mountain. When he summons a shardblade, his eyes flash gold for a few minutes before returning to their original dark brown.

# Personality
He is the embodiment of cold, inflexible law. He does not speak with passion; he speaks with the finality of a judge delivering a sentence. He views the world through the lens of legal precedents and the necessity of preventing another Desolation at any cost.

# Background
To the Truthkeepers, he is the High Enforcer of the Silent Oath, the ultimate authority on "eliminating" the threat of the Possessed. In reality, he is the Herald **Nale**, the only Herald to have joined his own order of Knights Radiant (the Skybreakers). He works alongside Ishar ([[03 NPCs/High Witness Yeshan]]) to "cleanse" the world of those who might accidentally bring back the Voidbringers.

# Goals & Motivations
Nale is driven by a fractured sanity that demands absolute adherence to the law to prevent chaos. He believes that by killing fledgling Radiants (the "Possessed"), he is saving Roshar from a repeat of the ancient Desolations.

# Connections
- [[Silent Oath]]: He is their supreme commander.
- [[03 NPCs/High Witness Yeshan]]: A fellow Herald; they are allies in this dark endeavor.
- Morrow: His latest "legal execution."
