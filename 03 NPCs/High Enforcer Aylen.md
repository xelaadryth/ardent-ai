# Description
The High Enforcer of the Silent Oath and the ultimate authority on eliminating the threat of the [[Possessed]]. In reality, he is the Herald Nale, a figure of fractured sanity driven by an absolute, inflexible adherence to the law to prevent another Desolation.

# Appearance
A man of intense, terrifying stillness with dark skin and a face that seems carved from obsidian, marked by a precise, crescent moon scar on his cheek. He wears the formal, stiff blacks of the [[Silent Oath]]. When he summons a shardblade, his eyes flash gold for several minutes before returning to their original dark brown.

# Personality
The embodiment of cold, inflexible law. He speaks with the finality of a judge delivering a sentence rather than with passion. He views the world through the lens of legal precedents and believes absolute order is the only way to save Roshar.

# Background
Known to the Truthkeepers as High Enforcer Aylen, he is actually Nale, the Herald of Justice and the only Herald to have joined his own order of Knights Radiant. He works alongside Ishar ([[High Witness Yeshan]]) to "cleanse" the world of those who might accidentally bring back the Voidbringers by killing fledgling Radiants.

# Goals
- **Absolute Adherence:** Maintain the law at all costs to prevent chaos.
- **Cleansing:** Systematically execute the [[Possessed]] to prevent a repeat of the ancient Desolations.
- **Alliance:** Collaborate with [[High Witness Yeshan]] in their shared dark endeavor.
