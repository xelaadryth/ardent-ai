# Description
A woman of intense focus and strict bearing who serves as the "brain" of House Sathir. She is a "correct" lighteyes who believes her duty is to protect those beneath her, often hiding her warmth behind a mask of high-noble duty.

# Appearance
She has pale green eyes and is in her mid-20s. She dresses elegantly in robes that show signs of careful repair—hand-sewn embroidery covering small tears, visible only to an expert. She often squints when thinking.

# Personality
Brusque, efficient, and deeply caring. She is exhausted by political pressure but refuses to break.

# Background
While her husband is away at the Shattered Plains, Marise manages the family estate and protects their people from the predatory politics of Citylord Nilar Yenev. She refuses to use slaves, relying instead on parshmen and paid darkeyes, which marks her as a target for traditionalist nobles.

# Goals
- **Protection:** Shield the children of her estate from conscription and the darkeyes from Yenev's sabotage.
- **Alliance:** Find reliable partners to help maintain House Sathir's standing and trade.
