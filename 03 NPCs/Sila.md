# Description
A large, imposing darkeyed man, crafty and dangerous. Marked by a diagonal scar across his lips. Currently at the Truthkeeper Camp.

# Personality
He acts like a simple thug who is just here for a quick buck, but is actually quite cunning. He likes drink and firemoss, which keeps him broke and unable to actually leave.

# Background
Faked his death after the Truthkeepers sentenced him to hard labor. He has a Ghostblood mark on his arm (via connection to other operatives). He is a skilled fighter and potentially could have been an Enforcer if he were reliable.

# Goals
- **Survival:** Avoid the Truthkeepers.
- **Wealth:** Secure payouts.
