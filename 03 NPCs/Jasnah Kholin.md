# Description
An Alethi princess and world-renowned scholar, widely known as a heretic for her rejection of Vorinism. She is secretly an Elsecaller of the Knights Radiant.

# Personality
Logical, cold, and intensely focused on her research. She brooks no fools but values those who demonstrate intellectual honesty.

# Background
The eldest daughter of King Gavilar Kholin, she has spent years researching the return of the Voidbringers. She has recently taken on a ward, [[Shallan Davar]], while staying in [[Kharbranth]] to utilize the Palanaeum.

# Goals
- Uncover the truth behind the ancient Desolations and the nature of the Voidbringers.
- Prepare for the return of the Knights Radiant.

# Connections
- [[Shallan Davar]]: Her ward and apprentice.
- [[Kabsal]]: An Ardent who has been attempting to befriend her in the Palanaeum.
