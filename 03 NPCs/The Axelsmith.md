# Description
The Axelsmith, formerly known as "The Axe", is a warrior who found a new purpose in life as a cartwright after his duel with [[Galeth-son-Thald]]. He speaks of himself in the third person.

# Personality
Humble, reflective, and speaks of himself in the third person. He no longer bases his self-worth solely on combat ability.

# Background
Formerly a fearsome warrior known as "The Axe". After losing a brutal duel to [[Galeth-son-Thald]] at Kesh's Gorge, he reflected on his life and realized he should not base all his self-worth on his combat ability. He re-evaluated his priorities and decided to go back to his roots as a simple cartwright, moving to [[Salimset]]. When the party passed by in [[011 Promotions and Revolar]], he fixed the wheel on their chull-pulled wagon free of charge and wished them the best.

# Goals
- Live a simple life as a cartwright in [[Salimset]].
- Apply his skills to repairing and building wagons.
- Avoid conflict and unnecessary violence.

# Connections
- [[Galeth-son-Thald]]: His opponent in a pivotal duel.
