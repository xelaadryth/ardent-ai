# Description
A man of sharp features and a polished, military bearing. He wears the embellished brown and gray of Highlord Paladar's favored commanders, often carrying a ceremonial blade that he is more than capable of using.

# Personality
Ambitious and ruthless. Odrin Lesh views the world as a game of territory and influence. He has little regard for the common folk of [[Revolar]], seeing them only as resources to be managed or suppressed.

# Background
A close friend of [[Highlord Paladar]], Odrin Lesh was installed as General of [[Revolar]] after [[General Spade]] was ousted. He is a primary architect of the region's current instability, secretly funding bandits in Sadeas lands to justify Paladar's "protection" and the northern expansion of the Vamah borders.

# Goals
- **Expansion:** Increase [[Highlord Paladar]]'s territory and his own influence by destabilizing neighboring princedoms.
- **Suppression:** Dismantle the [[Knights of Dusk]] and eradicate remaining loyalty to [[General Spade]].

# Connections
- [[Highlord Paladar]]: His patron and co-conspirator.
- [[General Spade]]: His predecessor and bitter rival.
- [[Truthkeepers]]: His primary allies in maintaining order and hunting "insurgents."
