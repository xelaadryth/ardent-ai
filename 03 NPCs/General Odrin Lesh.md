# Description
A man of sharp features and a polished, military bearing. He wears the embellished brown and gray of Highlord Paladar's favored commanders, representing the corrupt regime against the [[Knights of Dusk]].

# Personality
Ambitious and ruthless. Odrin Lesh views the world as a game of territory and influence. He has little regard for the common folk of [[Revolar]].

# Background
A close friend of [[Highlord Paladar]], Odrin Lesh was installed as General of [[Revolar]] after [[General Spade]] was ousted. He is a primary architect of the region's current instability.

# Goals
- **Consolidation:** Secure [[Revolar]] against the [[Knights of Dusk]].
- **Expansion:** Increase [[Highlord Paladar]]'s territory.

# Connections
- [[Highlord Paladar]]: His patron and co-conspirator.
- [[General Spade]]: His predecessor and bitter rival.
