# Description
A man of regal and commanding authority who possesses a visionary yet patient nature. In reality, he is the Herald Ishar, suffering from fractured sanity and conducting dangerous experiments with Connection to "fix" the world according to his own warped perspective. He is the Patron Herald of the Bondsmiths.

# Appearance
Regal and commanding, he radiates a natural authority. He is noted for his piercing eyes that seem to look through those he speaks to.

# Personality
A visionary leader who is immensely patient but brooks no dissent. He lacks empathy, viewing and treating his subordinates purely as tools to be utilized for his grander designs.

# Background
Known to many as High Witness Yeshan, he is the Herald Ishar. His sanity is severely fractured, leading him to experiment with the fundamental forces of Connection. Currently, he has largely abandoned his direct oversight of the Truthkeepers to lead holy wars in Tukkar under the identity of Tezim the Great.

# Goals
- **Agenda:** Utilize Worldhoppers to establish a specialized force for hunting the [[Possessed]], driven by a premonition of Odium’s ultimate victory.
- **Experimental Restoration:** Continue his experiments with Connection to reshape the world.
