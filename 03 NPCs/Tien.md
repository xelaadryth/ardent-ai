# Description
Tien is a young man with a bright, earnest face and curly hair often dusty from the front lines. He lacks the physical imposingness of his late brother, [[Kaladin]], but there is a resilient light in his eyes.

# Personality
Tien is naturally artistic and empathetic, often finding beauty in small things like interesting rocks or the way the light hits the stormwall. He is currently struggling with immense survivor's guilt, feeling that he is "living on borrowed time" bought by his brother's blood. However, he is possessed with strong determination to improve himself to not need others' protection, training the hardest out of everyone and rapidly becoming a force to be reckoned with.

# Background
After [[Kaladin]] died saving him in the Jah Keved border skirmishes, Tien threw himself into the military to honor his brother's sacrifice. He is a competent soldier but lacks the "killer instinct," often finding himself being protected by his squadmates—many of whom have died to keep him safe.

# Goals
- **Live Worthily:** To be worthy of the lives spent to keep him alive.
- **Self-Improvement:** Train to become a capable soldier who does not require constant protection.
