# Description
Tien is a young man with a bright, earnest face and curly hair often dusty from the front lines. He is known in the borderlands as the "Stormblessed" for his uncanny ability to survive impossible odds.

# Personality
Tien is naturally artistic and empathetic, often finding beauty in small things. While he once lacked the "killer instinct," his survival of the Jah Keved skirmishes and his brother's sacrifice have forged him into a resilient leader who collects those in need of protection.

# Background
After [[Kaladin]] died saving him in the Jah Keved border skirmishes—a sacrifice that seemingly passed on a unique destiny—Tien threw himself into the military. He is now a competent soldier and squad leader who defies death, drawing the attention of both the [[Truthkeepers]] and the [[Ghostbloods]].

# Goals
- **Live Worthily:** To be worthy of [[Kaladin]]'s sacrifice.
- **Protection:** Provide a shield for those under his command, including young recruits like [[Dillind]].

# Hooks
- **The Stormblessed Legend:** His reputation is growing, making him a beacon for potential Radiants and a target for the [[Silent Oath]].
- **Shardbearer Destiny:** He is destined to win a Shardblade in the coming skirmishes.
