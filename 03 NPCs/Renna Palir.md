# Description
An elegant Alethi lighteyes who is significantly more sympathetic than her peers in the [[Truthkeepers]]. She is driven by a protective nature and a deep, underlying sorrow. Currently investigating missing wagons in Hearthstone.

# Appearance
Bright green eyes and classic, elegant Alethi features. Moves elegantly, picks things up properly with her freehand, and her safehand is always demurely covered.

# Personality
Renna is kind, trusting, and protective. She has taken [[Iiko]] under her wing like a younger sibling and hopes she becomes an ardent. She harbors a secret delusion that her deceased younger sister is still alive.

# Background
Originally from [[Revolar]], she joined the Truthkeepers after witnessing their military logistics. She is currently in Hearthstone with [[Tahs]], two Herdazians, and [[Chip]]. She is perplexed by encounters with Vamah-uniformed bandits who steal nothing.

# Goals
- **Proving Worth:** To make a meaningful difference and prove her value to her parents.
- **Protecting [[Iiko]]:** Dedicated to her safety.
