# Description
[[Ardent Kaelis]] sees potential in [[Iiko]] and wishes to recruit them into the [[Devotary of Sincerity]] to ground their manic pursuit of truth through historical and geological discipline.

# Discovery
- **The Academic Challenge:** [[Ardent Kaelis]] presents [[Iiko]] with an ancient, illegible geological map and promises to teach the cipher only if they commit to the tenets of the [[Devotary of Sincerity]].
- **Library Encounters:** Finding Kaelis in [[The Vamah Archivum]], where he pointedly leaves open volumes detailing the [[Veristitalians]] and the "Truth of what happened."
- **Common Ground:** While discussing the [[Tablet of Eleven Cities]], Kaelis reveals he already knows of the "secret 11th city" and will share his research if [[Iiko]] adopts the formal path of an apprentice.
