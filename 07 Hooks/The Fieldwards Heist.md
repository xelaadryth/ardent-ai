# Description
A well-coordinated robbery of Warehouse 14 in the [[Fieldwards]] has left the [[Vamah Princedom]] administration in [[Revolar]] embarrassed and the [[Truthkeepers]] pressured to find the culprits. The theft of mundane necessities suggests the [[Knights of Dusk]] are not just arming a rebellion, but sustaining a growing population in hiding.

# Objectives
- Investigate the breach in the warehouse basement.
- Interview the scribes and guards involved in the incident.
- Trace the stolen goods back to the [[Knights of Dusk]] hideout in the [[Darkstone Forest]].
