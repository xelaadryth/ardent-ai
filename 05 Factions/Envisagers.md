# Description
A radical and secretive death cult based in the rural Alethi village of Talinan. They hold the fanatical belief that by forcing themselves and their children into life-threatening situations, they can induce the return of the Knights Radiant.

# Goals
- **Forced Radiance:** They believe the Lost Radiants will only return to those who demonstrate extreme resilience through hardship.
- **The Great Intervention:** They believe that during their trials, the Almighty or the Radiants will intervene to save the worthy.
- **Salvation:** Unlike common belief which paints the Radiants as traitors, the Envisagers view them as the only salvation against the coming darkness.

# Background
Originally hailing from the village of Talinar, the group was systematically hunted by the [[Truthkeepers]], who razed their village and suppressed the history of the event, claiming it to be the actions of the [[Possessed]].

# Structure
They operate in small, isolated cells hidden in plain sight within rural farming communities. Their fanaticism leads to dangerous and abusive practices, including child neglect and lethal trials.

# Allies & Rivals
- [[Truthkeepers]]: Primary rivals; the faction was hunted to near-extinction by them.
