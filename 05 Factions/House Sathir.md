# Description
A minor noble house of the Alethi [[Sadeas Princedom]], governing the eastern region near [[Stringken]] and [[Hobbleken]]. House Sathir is notable for its refusal to utilize slave labor, relying instead on paid darkeyes and parshmen.

# Leadership
- **[[Highlord Kethren Sathir]]**: The head of the house, currently serving as a Companylord at the Shattered Plains.
- **[[Highlady Marise Sathir]]**: Kethren's wife and current Regent, managing the estate and regional politics in his absence.

# Members & Residents
- **[[Shara Sathir]]**: The young daughter of Kethren and Marise.
- **[[Dalen]]**: The fiercely loyal darkeyed steward and veteran.
- **[[Mikeyel Franzay]]**: The head of the guard and protector of the Highlady.
- **Lumi**: The estate's house manager.
- **Ibsol**: The resident Ardent.

# Assets & Influence
The house manages a significant estate that serves as a sanctuary for orphans and those seeking refuge from the aggressive conscription tactics of neighboring lords like [[Citylord Nilar Yenev]].
