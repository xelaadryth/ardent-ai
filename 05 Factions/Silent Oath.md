# Overview
A shadow organization within the Truthkeepers, called in to eliminate high-priority "Possessed."

# Goals
- **Agenda:** Maintain "order" by eliminating fledgling Knights Radiant before the Desolation can return.
- **Current Posture:** Eliminating dangerous Possessed based on reports from Truthkeepers.

# Structure
- Reality: Members of the Skybreaker order.
- Leadership: High Enforcer [[High Enforcer Aylen]] (Herald Nale).

# Methods & Influence
Legalistic execution using Gravitation and Abrasion.

# Allies & Rivals
- Truthkeepers: Source of intelligence.
- Potential Radiants: Targets.
