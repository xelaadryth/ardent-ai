# Description
A shadow organization operating within the higher echelons of the [[Truthkeepers]], specialized in the clinical elimination of high-priority "Possessed" targets.

# Background
In reality, the Silent Oath is composed of members of the Skybreaker order. They leverage the legal and religious infrastructure of the Truthkeepers to identify fledgling Knights Radiant. Under the absolute leadership of [[High Enforcer Aylen]] (the Herald Nale), they hunt those who show signs of Surgebinding, believing that by eliminating these individuals, they can prevent the return of the Desolations.

# Goals
- **Maintain "Order":** Prevent the return of the Desolation by executing those who bond spren.
- **Strategic Purge:** Target and eliminate dangerous "Possessed" based on intelligence reports and legal precedents.

# Hooks
- **Shadow Pursuit:** The squad discovers they are being shadowed by a Silent Oath cell after a public display of Surgebinding.
- **The Enforcer’s Decree:** A high-profile execution is scheduled for a known associate of the party, justified by a convoluted legal technicality.

# Connections
- **[[Truthkeepers]]**: Their primary source of logistics and intelligence; the Silent Oath acts as the organization's "hidden blade."
- **Potential Radiants**: Viewed as targets and existential threats to be neutralized before they can progress in their Oaths.
- **[[High Enforcer Aylen]]**: The central authority and ideological leader of the group.
