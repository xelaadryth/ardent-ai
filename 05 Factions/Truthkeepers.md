# Overview
A secretive, militant organization claiming to be the only ones who truly understand the threat of the "Possessed."

# Goals
- **Agenda:** Identify, track, and neutralize individuals showing mental instability or hearing voices. They seek to secure Revolar to protect supply lines for militant Sadeas groups.
- **Current Posture:** Investigating and hunting down the [[Knights of Dusk]] to fortify supply lines from Vamah Princedom.

# Structure
- Silent Oath (secret power at the top)
- [[High Witness Yeshan]] - The spiritual leader, secretly the Herald Ishar.
- Adjudicators - Manage high-level plans.
- Enforcers - Hunt and kill Possessed.
- Witnesses - Investigate rumors and logistics.

# Methods & Influence
Operate with autonomy via political cover from the Princedom of Vamah.

# Allies & Rivals
- Knights Radiant: Potential rivals; seen as "Possessed."
