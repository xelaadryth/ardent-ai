# Description
A secretive, militant organization that operates under the conviction that they alone understand the existential threat posed by the [[Possessed]]. They provide a front for more dangerous entities while maintaining a firm grip on local politics and security.

The Truthkeepers operate with near-total autonomy, shielded by the political cover of the Princedom of Vamah. While they present themselves as protectors of the peace, they are a primary tool for suppressing the return of the Knights Radiant. Their spiritual guidance comes from [[High Witness Yeshan]], though few realize his true identity as the Herald Ishar.

# Goals
- **Neutralization:** Identify and "neutralize" individuals showing signs of Surgebinding or mental instability.
- **Logistical Control:** Secure [[Revolar]] and fortify supply lines for militant Sadeas groups coming from the Vamah Princedom.
- **Suppression:** Actively hunt down and dismantle the [[Knights of Dusk]] to ensure regional stability.

# Structure
*   **[[Silent Oath]]**: The secret power at the apex, directing the organization's most lethal activities.
*   **[[High Witness Yeshan]]**: The spiritual figurehead and ultimate authority.
*   **Adjudicators**: Senior officers who manage high-level strategy and administrative justice.
*   **Enforcers**: The militant arm tasked with hunting and killing the Possessed.
*   **Witnesses**: Investigators responsible for tracking rumors and managing logistics.

# Allies & Rivals
- [[Vamah]]: Patrons who fund them to cause chaos in Sadeas Princedom.
- [[Knights of Dusk]]: Org they were hired to eliminate.
