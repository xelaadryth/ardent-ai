# Overview
A dangerous criminal organization operating across Roshar and branching into interstellar trade. Their main base of operations is in the Veden city of Bodenar, while they maintain a strategic foothold in the Horneater Peaks to facilitate inter-planetary smuggling via a local Perpendicularity.

# Goals & Agenda
The Bloodmarks prioritize the smuggling of resources—specifically Nurian gems—between Mythos and Roshar. Their illicit activities include:
- **Firemoss Trade:** Distribution and sales.
- **Forgery:** Creating false identities, including those of deceased lesser-house lighteyes from the far west (e.g., House Charash).
- **Smuggling:** Movement of luxury goods, spheres, and darkwood. They utilize complex logistics, including wagon-to-barge transfers upriver of [[Branchken]] and [[Leafken]], protected by foreign mercenaries.
- **Political Influence:** Supplying [[Highlord Paladar]] with luxury goods, firemoss, and spheres in exchange for protection and a blind eye to their activities.
- **Debt Collection:** Ruthless intimidation of merchants, physical mutilation, and abduction to fuel their slave trade.
- **Arms Dealing:** Trafficking official Vamah military armaments.

# Structure
The organization is strictly hierarchical:
1. **Grand Justicars:** The four supreme leaders (e.g., [[Grand Justicar Malice]]).
2. **Templars:** High-ranking overseers of the Arbiters.
3. **Arbiters:** Lieutenants and the highest rank of common soldiers.
4. **Lashes:** Sergeants.
5. **Thugs:** The rank-and-file muscle.

# Methods & Influence
Ruthless and efficient, the Bloodmarks leverage their knowledge of worldhopping and the Perpendicularity to bypass local law. They have successfully bribed approximately 50% of the [[Revolar]] guard. Their operations have made them a primary enemy of the [[Ghostbloods]].

# Allies & Rivals
- **[[Highlord Paladar]]:** A political beneficiary of their smuggling and bribery.
- **[[N'tal]]:** A target of their past operations; he holds a deep-seated personal vendetta against them.
- **[[Ghostbloods]]:** Their primary criminal rivals; the two groups are in a state of open shadow war.
- **[[Truthkeepers]]:** An uneasy potential partner; though the organizations have not yet formally collaborated, the Bloodmarks are open to an arrangement.
- **[[Knights of Dusk]]:** A target of Bloodmark counter-operations; the Bloodmarks avoid aiding them to remain in the city's good graces.

# Current Posture
Currently thriving in the Horneater Peaks, utilizing the remote terrain to maintain their inter-planetary smuggling ring undetected.
