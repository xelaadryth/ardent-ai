# Overview
The Vamah Archivum is the largest public library in [[Revolar]]. Established in the decades following the Hierocracy, it was built to centralize knowledge under the oversight of the princedom's legal scholars. While it is highly respectable, it is not particularly renowned throughout Roshar, as its collection is notoriously curated. Unknown to the public, it serves as the primary regional headquarters for the [[Truthkeepers]], with its restricted lower levels housing their command center.

# Atmosphere
The Archivum is bright, clean, and smells of fresh ink and cedar. Unlike older libraries that feel heavy with age, the Archivum feels modern and efficient. "Librarians" patrol the aisles with soft-soled boots. There is a palpable sense of "correctness" here; everything is in its proper place, and the knowledge is carefully guarded.

# Notable Features
- **The Gallery of Betrayal:** A prominent wing of the library dedicated to "Historical Truths," containing books and tapestries that detail the "treachery" of the ancient Knights Radiant.
- **Missing Books:** In the history, theology, and philosophy sections, there are a lot of gaps that have never been filled.
- **The Command Sanctum:** Accessible only through a series of secret passwords and a disguised entrance behind a shelf of Veden law books, this underground facility houses the Truthkeepers tactical operations.
- **The Cipher:**
  - Visitor: “Law is the chain that binds.”
  - Librarian: “Truth is the light that blinds.”
  - Visitor: “What do they protect?”
  - Librarian: “They protect us.” (Higher-ranking librarians add: "All of us.")
  - Visitor (High-ranked): “But we decide the truth.”

# Inhabitants
- **Head Archivist Valas:** A high-ranking Truthkeeper who oversees both the public library and the secret censorship efforts.
- [[Renna Palir]]: Often seen in the Command Sanctum managing logistics and assisting Enforcers with entry.

# Secrets
The library's collection is essentially an engine of propaganda. Every book within its walls has been edited or selected to reinforce the idea that the [[Possessed]] are a modern manifestation of an ancient evil that the Radiants once brought upon the world. Access to the Command Sanctum is granted only to those of Enforcer rank and above.
