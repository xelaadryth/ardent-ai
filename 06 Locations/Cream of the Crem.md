# Overview
The "premier" lodging in the Driftwards district of [[06 Locations/Revolar]]. While significantly more expensive than its neighbors at 4 marks a night, its reputation for being "clean" is relative to the surrounding slums.

# Atmosphere
The inn smells faintly of vinegar and scrub-soap, a constant battle against the encroaching crem and the dampness of the district. It tries to maintain a veneer of respectability with polished (if scarred) wooden tables and a dining hall that is actually free of Chull-dung odor.

# Notable Features
- **The "Clean" Dining Hall:** Known for serving hot tuber-stew and bread that isn't completely stale.
- **Shared Balconies:** The upper-floor rooms share narrow stone balconies that offer a view of the Driftwards' rooftops—perfect for quiet, private conversations.
- **The Storm-Wall:** A thick, reinforced stone wall on the windward side that makes the inn one of the few places in the district that doesn't rattle during a Highstorm.

# Inhabitants
Merchants, minor functionaries of the Vamah Princedom, and travelers who can afford to pay for a modicum of safety.

# Secrets
The innkeep, a portly man named **Lukan**, takes a "no questions asked" approach to his guests' business, provided their marks are genuine. He has a secret cellar used for storing high-quality Horneater white, which he only breaks out for "special" guests.
