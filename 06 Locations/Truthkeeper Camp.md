# Description
A fortified military encampment situated against a cliffside, serving as a primary training and mobilization hub for the [[Truthkeepers]]. The perimeter is notably marked by erratic, "creeping trees" that move independently of the wind.

# Background
The camp serves as the initiation point for new recruits and a staging ground for investigations into the [[Possessed]]. It is under the high-level oversight of [[High Witness Yeshan]] and the direct administrative command of [[Adjudicator Peton]]. The atmosphere is one of rigid discipline and unsettling transition, often haunted by the "distorted dreams" of those arriving from afar.
