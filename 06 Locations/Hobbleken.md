# Description
A small town sheltered in the leeward side of hills within a lait, known for its agriculture and Hobble's Brook.

# Background
Managed by a local official who is wary of lighteyes. The economy revolves around produce, herbs, and farming. It is currently a site of tension regarding land ownership rights between farmers and squatters.
