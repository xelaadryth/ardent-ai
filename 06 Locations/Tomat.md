# Overview
A rural Alethi settlement situated on the edge of the [[Sadeas]] Highprincedom, near the border of Jah Keved. It is characterized by its terraced farms and its strategic position as a recruitment hub for the border wars.

# Background
Tomat has traditionally been a quiet agricultural community. However, its proximity to the volatile Veden border has transformed it into a critical military staging ground. It is the primary site of [[Amaram]]'s current recruitment drives, where young boys are drafted into the "Stormblessed" squads. The village is caught in the tension between its loyalty to the Alethi crown and the growing predatory incursions from [[Vamah]] and other Veden interests.

# Notable Features
- **The Recruitment Green:** A central, muddy square dominated by the Pavilions of [[Amaram]]. This is where the "Trial of the Spear" takes place for new recruits.
- **The Border Terraces:** Intricate stone-and-soil terraces built to withstand highstorms, used for growing hardy grains. They offer a vantage point into the nearby Jah Keved hills.
- **The Whispering Stone:** A partially buried, ancient obsidian pillar in the village outskirts. It vibrates with a low, dissonant hum whenever a Highstorm is approaching or when an active Radiant bond is nearby, a phenomenon the locals view with superstition.

# Inhabitants
- [[Amaram]]: Currently overseeing recruitment and military logistics.
- [[Tien]]: A squad leader and localized hero known as the "Stormblessed."
- [[Dillind]]: A young survivor seeking a new life under Tien's protection.
- [[Helaran]]: A mysterious figure rumored to be stalking the camp.

# Secrets
- **The Shadow Path:** Beneath the Whispering Stone lies a natural cavern system that leads across the border, used by smugglers and potentially by the [[Ghostbloods]] to move goods undetected.
- **Unstable Recruitment:** Some of the youths "recruited" here are actually being sold to [[Bloodmarks]] labor camps by corrupt subordinates within the Sadeas army.

# Hooks
- **The Stone's Resonance:** [[Viscose]] or [[Dreamwaker]] may be drawn to the Whispering Stone, sensing it is a piece of a larger, broken fabrial or an ancient boundary marker.
- **Assassination Attempt:** The party might witness [[Helaran]]'s scouting of the camp, forcing them to choose between protecting [[Amaram]] or allowing the assassin to strike.
