# Overview
A sprawling, chaotic open-air market in the heart of the Driftwards. It is the primary hub for foreign trade in Revolar, specializing in "exotic" goods from across Roshar—and occasionally beyond.

# Atmosphere
The bazaar is a sensory overload of scents, sounds, and colors. The air is a mix of pungent spices, wet stone, and the shouting of merchants in half a dozen languages. Rickety stalls are draped in vibrant cloths, and the muddy ground is partially covered by mismatched rugs and wooden planks.

# Notable Features
- **The Distant Row:** A section of the market known for selling goods that appeal to those from distant lands, such as Shin grass-cloth or strange, non-Rosharan seeds.
- **The Junk-Heaps:** Large piles of miscellaneous items where a keen eye might find discarded technology or ancient artifacts.

# Inhabitants
A melting pot of Alethi, Vedens, Thaylens, and more mysterious travelers. It is a place where social rank matters less than the weight of one's purse.

# Secrets
A merchant named **Zabriel** occasionally deals in rare, fake-sounding items from off-world, including fragments of atium, Taldain whitesand, and Nurian diamond broams. These items are of great interest to the [[Undertaker]], who collects such off-world trinkets.
