# Overview
A damp, dimly lit shop tucked away in a quiet corner of the Driftwards. It is the most comprehensive source of medicinal herbs, poultices, and alchemical ingredients in the district.

# Atmosphere
The air inside is thick with the earthy scent of drying moss and the sharp tang of vinegar. Bunches of herbs hang from the low ceiling, and the walls are lined with jars of powders and liquids in every color. It is quiet and cool, a stark contrast to the noise of the district outside.

# Notable Features
- **The Herb Garden:** A small, magically illuminated cellar where Mother Tane grows rare specimens that require specific lighting.
- **The Poison Cabinet:** A locked shelf containing dangerous substances, accessible only to those Mother Tane trusts.
- **The Consultation Nook:** A private corner where the injured can receive treatment without being seen by the guard patrols.

# Inhabitants
- **Mother Tane:** The elderly, sharp-tongued apothecary who runs the shop. She is a master of medicine and doesn't care about a customer's faction, only their health (and their marks).

# Secrets
Mother Tane hides a rare specimen of "grass" in her attic—a Shin plant that doesn't move.
